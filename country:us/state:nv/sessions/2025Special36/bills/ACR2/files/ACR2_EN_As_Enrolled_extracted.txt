Section 1:
Assembly Concurrent Resolution No. 2–Committee
on Legislative Operations and Elections
FILE NUMBER..........
ASSEMBLY CONCURRENT RESOLUTION—Authorizing
certain reimbursement for travel.
SENATE CONCURRING, That if any amount of reimbursement for
travel that a Legislator is authorized to use for serving during the
83rd (2025) Session of the Nevada Legislature is unused, the unused
reimbursement shall be administratively applied by the Legislative
Counsel Bureau to reimburse the travel expenses of that Legislator
for serving in any special session held during the period beginning
on June 3, 2025, and ending on November 3, 2026, that exceed the
maximum amount of reimbursement authorized for that Legislator
for serving during a special session pursuant to NRS 218A.645; and
be it further
RESOLVED, That except as otherwise provided in this resolution,
if any authorized amount of reimbursement for travel by a
Legislator is unused following any regular or special session, the
unused reimbursement shall be administratively applied by the
Legislative Counsel Bureau to reimburse the travel expenses of any
other Legislator who has exceeded the maximum amount of
reimbursement authorized for that Legislator; and be it further
RESOLVED, That if the unused reimbursement for all Legislators
has been fully claimed, a Legislator who has exceeded the
maximum amount of reimbursement is responsible for paying such
excess costs of his or her travel expenses; and be it further
RESOLVED, That the amount of any claim for reimbursement
that is approved be verified, processed and paid in the same manner
as claims pursuant to NRS 218A.645; and be it further
RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
adoption.
