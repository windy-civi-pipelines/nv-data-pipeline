security. (BDR R-6)

EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
ASSEMBLY CONCURRENT RESOLUTION—Authorizing
certain reimbursement for Legislator security.
1 RESOLVED BY THE ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, THE
2 SENATE CONCURRING, That to offset the cost of personal security
3 related to serving as a member of the Legislature, a Legislator may,
4 upon consultation with the Legislative Police, apply for
5 reimbursement for such costs during the period beginning on
6 November 6, 2024, and ending on November 3, 2026, by submitting
7 a claim for the reimbursement of any amount the Legislator is
8 required to pay for personal security related to serving as a member
9 of the Legislature, up to a maximum amount of $5,000 per
10 Legislator; and be it further
11 RESOLVED, That the amount of any such claim that is approved
12 be verified, processed and paid in the same manner as claims
13 pursuant to NRS 218A.150; and be it further
14 RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
15 adoption.
H
