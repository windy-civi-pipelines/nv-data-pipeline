Section 1:
Assembly Concurrent Resolution No. 3–Committee
on Legislative Operations and Elections
FILE NUMBER..........
ASSEMBLY CONCURRENT RESOLUTION—Authorizing
certain reimbursement for Legislator security.
SENATE CONCURRING, That to offset the cost of personal security
related to serving as a member of the Legislature, a Legislator may,
upon consultation with the Legislative Police, apply for
reimbursement for such costs during the period beginning on
November 6, 2024, and ending on November 3, 2026, by submitting
a claim for the reimbursement of any amount the Legislator is
required to pay for personal security related to serving as a member
of the Legislature, up to a maximum amount of $5,000 per
Legislator; and be it further
RESOLVED, That the amount of any such claim that is approved
be verified, processed and paid in the same manner as claims
pursuant to NRS 218A.150; and be it further
RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
adoption.
