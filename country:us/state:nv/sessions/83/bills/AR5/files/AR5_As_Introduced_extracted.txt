A.R. 5
ASSEMBLY RESOLUTION NO. 5–ASSEMBLYMEMBERS
YEAGER, JAUREGUI AND HAFEN
MAY 7, 2025
____________
Read and Adopted
SUMMARY—Adds former Assemblymember Odis “Tyrone”
Thompson to the Assembly Wall of Distinction.
(BDR R-1162)
~
ASSEMBLY RESOLUTION—Adding former Assemblymember
Odis “Tyrone” Thompson to the Assembly Wall of
Distinction.
1 WHEREAS, The Assembly of the Legislature of the State of
2 Nevada has established a Wall of Distinction for those past
3 members of the Assembly selected by leadership who served with
4 great distinction and made exemplary contributions to the State of
5 Nevada; and
6 WHEREAS, Assemblymember Odis “Tyrone” Thompson was
7 born on September 30, 1967, in Las Vegas, Nevada, to Odis and
8 Vertis Thompson; and
9 WHEREAS, After graduating from Valley High School in Las
10 Vegas in 1985, he earned a bachelor’s degree in criminal justice
11 from Northern Arizona University in 1989 and a master’s degree in
12 organizational management from the University of Phoenix in 2010;
13 and
14 WHEREAS, Spanning three decades, Tyrone’s career in Nevada
15 was dedicated to public service and included work in various
16 positions in local and state government and service on boards and as
17 a volunteer for many important causes, including as a Court
18 Appointed Special Advocate for abused and neglected children in
19 foster care for over 17 years; and
20 WHEREAS, When Tyrone was appointed to the Nevada
21 Assembly to fill a vacancy during the 2013 Legislative Session, he
22 took the opportunity to study the issues and discovered a passion for
-
*AR5*
– 2 –
1 representing his constituents, especially underrepresented persons,
2 including homeless and vulnerable persons; and
3 WHEREAS, Tyrone’s hard work was rewarded when he was
4 subsequently elected to serve three more terms representing
5 Assembly District 17; and
6 WHEREAS, During his tenure at the Nevada Legislature, Tyrone
7 championed many significant legislative measures, including
8 legislation strengthening and expanding mentoring programs,
9 seeking educational opportunities for pupils, addressing issues faced
10 by homeless youth, dealing with important health care issues,
11 addressing the reintegration of criminal offenders and designating
12 Peace Week in the State of Nevada; and
13 WHEREAS, Tyrone served on various committees and held many
14 positions at the Legislature, including being selected to serve during
15 the 2019 Legislative Session as Assembly Majority Whip and Chair
16 of the Assembly Committee on Education; and
17 WHEREAS, Tyrone was a leader in his community, a role model
18 and a selfless, hardworking public servant; and
19 WHEREAS, After his passing in 2019, Tyrone’s commitment to
20 youth development and educational opportunities has been
21 memorialized through the establishment of Ty’s Place, a safe and
22 enriching after-school hub in North Las Vegas for teens; and
23 WHEREAS, The Board of Regents of the University of Nevada
24 fittingly honored Tyrone’s dedication to higher education by naming
25 the new student union building at the North Las Vegas Campus of
26 the College of Southern Nevada as the Tyrone Thompson Student
27 Union; and
28 WHEREAS, The Clark County School District has also honored
29 Tyrone’s legacy by establishing the Tyrone Thompson Elementary
30 School in Las Vegas, Nevada; and
31 WHEREAS, In honor of its alumnus, the University of Phoenix
32 has established a full-tuition scholarship in Tyrone’s name that is
33 awarded annually to a Nevada resident who is pursuing an
34 undergraduate degree; and
35 WHEREAS, Known for his distinctive style and vibrant
36 personality, Tyrone held a special affection for the color purple,
37 often incorporating the color into his wardrobe and surroundings as
38 a symbol of his creativity, wisdom and unwavering commitment to
39 bringing beauty and inspiration to the lives of those around him; and
40 WHEREAS, Above all else, Tyrone loved his family and made
41 certain his mother knew he held her dear, sending her flowers every
42 Monday that he could not be with her while he was serving in the
43 Legislature; and
44 WHEREAS, Tyrone’s famous, heartwarming smile will be long
45 remembered as the embodiment of his character, and his tireless
-
*AR5*
– 3 –
1 advocacy, compassion for others, leadership, dedication and work
2 ethic will continue to serve as an inspiration; now, therefore, be it
3 RESOLVED BY THE ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, That
4 for his outstanding service in the Assembly and his dedication to the
5 people of this State, Odis “Tyrone” Thompson is hereby added to
6 the Assembly Wall of Distinction; and be it further
7 RESOLVED, That the Chief Clerk of the Assembly prepare and
8 transmit a copy of this resolution to the family of former
9 Assemblymember Tyrone Thompson; and be it further
10 RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
11 adoption.
H
