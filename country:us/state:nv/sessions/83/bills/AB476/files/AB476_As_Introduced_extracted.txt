Section 1:
A.B. 476
MARCH 19, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to the Thomas & Mack
Legal Clinic at the William S. Boyd School of Law
of the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
(BDR S-1140)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Thomas & Mack Legal
Clinic at the William S. Boyd School of Law of the
University of Nevada, Las Vegas, for the purpose of
providing pro bono legal services; and providing other
matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Thomas & Mack Legal Clinic at the William S.
3 Boyd School of Law of the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, for
4 the purpose of providing pro bono legal services the following
5 sums:
6 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................. $500,000
7 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 .................................. $500,000
8 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1
9 remaining at the end of the respective fiscal years must not be
10 committed for expenditure after June 30 of the respective fiscal
11 years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
12 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
13 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
14 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
15 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively, by
-
*AB476*
– 2 –
1 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
2 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and
3 must be reverted to the State General Fund on or before
4 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively.
5 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
