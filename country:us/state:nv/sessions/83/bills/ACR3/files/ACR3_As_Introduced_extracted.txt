ASSEMBLY CONCURRENT RESOLUTION—Expressing
gratitude to the National Conference of State Legislatures
for 50 years of service.
1 WHEREAS, The National Conference of State Legislatures was
2 founded in 1975 and has evolved over the last 50 years to become
3 the premier organization solely dedicated to serving the legislators
4 and legislative staff of the 50 states, commonwealths and territories
5 of the United States and the District of Columbia; and
6 WHEREAS, The National Conference of State Legislatures was
7 created from the merger of three organizations that served or
8 represented the interest of legislators and legislative staff and that
-
*ACR3*
– 2 –
1 shared the belief that legislative service is one of democracy’s
2 worthiest pursuits; and
3 WHEREAS, The National Conference of State Legislatures is a
4 bipartisan organization that seeks to advance the effectiveness,
5 independence and integrity of state legislatures, foster interstate
6 communication and cooperation and ensure states have a strong,
7 cohesive voice in the federal governmental system; and
8 WHEREAS, The state legislatures of this nation are laboratories
9 of democracy and have continually demonstrated their unique
10 ability to adeptly tackle emergent challenges by bringing together
11 people from different backgrounds and communities to find
12 common ground; and
13 WHEREAS, The National Conference of State Legislatures
14 facilitates the exchange of ideas, provides critical research and
15 information, encourages a rigorous review of the complex issues
16 confronting our communities, states and nation and strives to
17 strengthen the bond between state legislatures in the United States
18 and the international community; and
19 WHEREAS, The National Conference of State Legislatures
20 celebrated its 50th anniversary on January 1, 2025; now, therefore,
21 be it
22 RESOLVED BY THE ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, THE
23 SENATE CONCURRING, That the members of the 83rd Session of the
24 Nevada Legislature recognize and commend the National
25 Conference of State Legislatures for its exceptional leadership and
26 commitment to the legislative institution and specifically its service
27 to the Nevada Legislature as it celebrates its 50th anniversary; and
28 be it further
29 RESOLVED, That the Chief Clerk of the Assembly prepare and
30 transmit a copy of this resolution to the Chief Executive Officer of
31 the National Conference of State Legislatures; and be it further
32 RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
33 adoption.
