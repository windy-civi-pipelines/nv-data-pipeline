Section 1:
S.B. 506
MAY 28, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Interim Finance
Committee for allocation to the State Public Charter
School Authority to provide money to charter
schools for salary increases for certain employees.
(BDR S-1249)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Interim Finance Committee
for allocation to the State Public Charter School Authority
to provide money to charter schools for salary increases
for certain employees; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Interim Finance Committee for allocation to the
3 State Public Charter School Authority to provide money to charter
4 schools for salary increases for teachers and education support
5 professionals the following sums:
6 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ............................. $19,314,297
7 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ............................. $19,314,297
8 2. On or before June 15, 2025, each charter school in this State
9 shall submit to the State Public Charter School Authority a
10 statement of the number of teachers and education support
11 professionals employed by the charter school on October 1, 2024.
12 The State Public Charter School Authority shall compile this
13 information and, not later than July 15, 2025:
-
*SB506*
– 2 –
1 (a) Submit the compiled information to the Interim Finance
2 Committee.
3 (b) Estimate the total amount of money which the Authority will
4 be authorized to distribute to each charter school for Fiscal Year
5 2025-2026 pursuant to subsection 7 and provide this information to
6 each charter school.
7 3. On or before June 15, 2026, each charter school in this State
8 shall submit to the State Public Charter School Authority a
9 statement of the number of teachers and education support
10 professionals employed by the charter school on October 1, 2025.
11 The State Public Charter School Authority shall compile this
12 information and, not later than July 15, 2026:
13 (a) Submit the compiled information to the Interim Finance
14 Committee.
15 (b) Estimate the total amount of money which the Authority will
16 be authorized to distribute to each charter school for Fiscal Year
17 2026-2027 pursuant to subsection 7 and provide this information to
18 each charter school.
19 4. On or before August 15, 2025, and August 15, 2026, each
20 charter school in this State shall submit to the State Public Charter
21 School Authority a plan by the charter school to increase salaries for
22 teachers and education support professionals during the then-current
23 fiscal year which:
24 (a) Describes the manner in which money distributed to the
25 charter school will be allocated among the teachers and education
26 support professionals employed by the charter school; and
27 (b) Provides for an increase in salary for teachers and education
28 support professionals which does not replace or supplant any other
29 form of compensation which was provided to teachers or education
30 support professionals of the charter school before Fiscal Year 2025-
31 2026 or which was bargained for or planned for Fiscal Year 2025-
32 2026 or any succeeding fiscal year before the effective date of this
33 act.
34 5. On or before September 15, 2025, and September 15, 2026,
35 the State Public Charter School Authority shall compile and submit
36 to the Interim Finance Committee the plans submitted by each
37 charter school for the then-current fiscal year pursuant to
38 subsection 4.
39 6. Money appropriated by subsection 1 may only be allocated
40 to the State Public Charter School Authority by the Interim Finance
41 Committee upon request by the Authority after the Authority has
42 complied with the requirements of subsection 2 or 3, as applicable,
43 and subsection 5.
-
*SB506*
– 3 –
1 7. The amount of money distributed by the State Public Charter
2 School Authority to a charter school pursuant to this section must
3 not exceed an amount which is equal to:
4 (a) For Fiscal Year 2025-2026, $19,314,297 multiplied by a
5 percentage which is the number of teachers and education support
6 professionals employed by the charter school on October 1, 2024,
7 expressed as a percentage of the total number of teachers and
8 education support professionals employed in all of the charter
9 schools in this State on October 1, 2024.
10 (b) For Fiscal Year 2026-2027, $19,314,297 multiplied by a
11 percentage which is the number of teachers and education support
12 professionals employed by the charter school on October 1, 2025,
13 expressed as a percentage of the total number of teachers and
14 education support professionals employed in all of the charter
15 schools in this State on October 1, 2025.
16 8. Money appropriated by subsection 1 may only be distributed
17 by the Authority to a charter school which has complied with the
18 requirements of subsection 2 or 3, as applicable, and subsection 4.
19 9. The money appropriated by subsection 1 must be used to
20 supplement and not supplant or cause to be reduced any other source
21 of funding for the support of charter schools or for the salary and
22 compensation of teachers or education support professionals
23 employed by any charter school.
24 10. On or before August 1, 2026, and August 1, 2027, each
25 charter school in this State that received a distribution from the
26 money appropriated by subsection 1 during Fiscal Year 2025-2026
27 and Fiscal Year 2026-2027, respectively, shall submit a report to the
28 State Public Charter School Authority which includes, without
29 limitation:
30 (a) Detailed information on how all the money received pursuant
31 to this section was spent by the charter school during the
32 immediately preceding fiscal year; and
33 (b) The actual increase to the salary of teachers and education
34 support professionals employed by the charter school during the
35 immediately preceding fiscal year and the amount of such an
36 increase for which the money received pursuant to this section was
37 spent.
38 11. On or before October 1, 2026, and October 1, 2027, the
39 State Public Charter School Authority shall compile and submit to
40 the Interim Finance Committee the reports received by the Authority
41 pursuant to subsection 10 with respect to the immediately preceding
42 fiscal year.
43 12. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1
44 remaining at the end of the respective fiscal years must not be
45 committed for expenditure after June 30 of the respective fiscal
-
*SB506*
– 4 –
1 years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
2 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
3 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
4 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
5 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively, by
6 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
7 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and
8 must be reverted to the State General Fund on or before
9 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively.
10 13. As used in this section:
11 (a) “Administrator” means a person who spends at least 50
12 percent of his or her work year supervising other staff or licensed
13 personnel, or both, and who is not classified by the governing body
14 of the charter school as a professional-technical employee.
15 (b) “Education support professional” means a person, other than
16 a teacher or administrator, who is employed to work at a charter
17 school, including, without limitation:
18 (1) Paraprofessionals;
19 (2) School police officers, school resource officers and other
20 providers of security services at a school;
21 (3) School nurses;
22 (4) School counselors;
23 (5) School psychologists;
24 (6) School social workers;
25 (7) Drivers of school buses;
26 (8) Secretaries;
27 (9) Members of the custodial or maintenance staff; and
28 (10) Workers in food services.
29 (c) “Teacher” means a person licensed pursuant to chapter 391
30 of NRS who is classified by the governing body of a charter school:
31 (1) As a teacher and who spends at least 50 percent of his or
32 her work year providing instruction or discipline to pupils; or
33 (2) As instructional support staff, who does not hold a
34 supervisory position and who spends not more than 50 percent of
35 his or her work year providing instruction to pupils. Such
36 instructional support staff includes, without limitation, librarians
37 and persons who provide instructional support.
38 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
