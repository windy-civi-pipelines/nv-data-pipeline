EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, School
of Dental Medicine for the creation of a cleft and craniofacial medical
team; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, School of Dental Medicine the sum of
3 [$2,900,000] $1,314,000 for the creation of a cleft and craniofacial medical team.
4 The team shall:
5 (a) Obtain approval as a Cleft Palate and Craniofacial Team from the American
6 Cleft Palate Craniofacial Association, or its successor organization;
7 (b) Provide cleft and craniofacial health care services through a clinic that is
8 open:
9 (1) At least 4 hours for at least 1 day each month; and
10 (2) If the team determines there is a need and that resources are available,
11 at least 8 hours for at least 2 days each month;
12 (c) Provide services to:
13 (1) Children who are less than 3 years of age; and
14 (2) If the team determines that resources are available, children with
15 special needs who are at least 3 years of age but less than 18 years of age;
Senate Amendment No. 846 to Senate Bill No. 280 Page 4
1 (d) Establish partnerships with other institutions of higher education in this
2 State and any other organization that may assist the team in complying with this
3 subsection.
4 2. Any remaining balance of an appropriation made by this section must not
5 be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
6 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the appropriation is
7 granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
8 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
9 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the
10 money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State
11 General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
12 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
