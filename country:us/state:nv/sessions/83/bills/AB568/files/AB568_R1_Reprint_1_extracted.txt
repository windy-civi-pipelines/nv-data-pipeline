FIRST REPRINT A.B. 568
ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 568–COMMITTEE ON WAYS AND MEANS
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 14, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Nevada System of
Higher Education for operational and instructional
expenses. (BDR S-1201)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Nevada System of Higher
Education for operational and instructional expenses; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
3 $22,441,996 for operational and instructional expenses of the
4 University of Nevada, Reno.
5 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
6 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
7 $23,580,944 for operational and instructional expenses of the
8 University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
9 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
10 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
11 $113,154 for operational and instructional expenses of the
12 Agricultural Experiment Station.
13 Sec. 4. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
14 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
15 $277,476 for the Cooperative Extension Service budget account for
-
*AB568_R1*
– 2 –
1 operational and instructional expenses of the Cooperative Extension
2 Service.
3 Sec. 5. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
4 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
5 $867,708 for the UNLV Law School budget account for operational
6 and instructional expenses of the William S. Boyd School of Law at
7 the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
8 Sec. 6. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
9 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
10 $2,124,972 for the UNLV Dental School budget account for
11 operational and instructional expenses of the School of Dental
12 Medicine at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
13 Sec. 7. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
14 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
15 $1,072,226 for operational and instructional expenses of the Nevada
16 State University.
17 Sec. 8. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
18 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
19 $5,332,632 for operational and instructional expenses of the College
20 of Southern Nevada.
21 Sec. 9. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
22 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
23 $408,738 for operational and instructional expenses of Great Basin
24 College.
25 Sec. 10. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
26 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
27 $221,370 for operational and instructional expenses of Western
28 Nevada College.
29 Sec. 11. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
30 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of
31 $1,043,258 for operational and instructional expenses of Truckee
32 Meadows Community College.
33 Sec. 12. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
34 Fund to the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of $2,976
35 for the Prison Education Program budget account for operational
36 and instructional expenses of the Prison Education Program.
37 Sec. 13. Any remaining balance of the appropriations made by
38 sections 1 to 12, inclusive, of this act must not be committed for
39 expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
40 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the
41 appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and
42 any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be spent
43 for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to
44 which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the money
-
*AB568_R1*
– 3 –
1 was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the
2 State General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
3 Sec. 13.5. 1. The Legislature hereby declares its intent that
4 the appropriations made pursuant to this act are one-time grants of
5 money intended to sustain the operations of the Nevada System of
6 Higher Education and the institutions named herein for the duration
7 of the 2025-2027 biennium.
8 2. The Legislature hereby urges the Nevada System of Higher
9 Education, during the 2025-2027 biennium, to seek additional
10 sources of revenue to ensure that the System is able to pay its
11 operational and instructional expenses in future biennia.
12 Sec. 14. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
