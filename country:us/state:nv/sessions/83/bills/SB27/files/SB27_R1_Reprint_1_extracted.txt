SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to museums.

EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to museums; changing the name of the Nevada
State Museum to the Nevada State Museum Carson City;
revising the powers and duties of the Board of Museums
and History; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law establishes certain museums and historical societies as institutions
2 of the Division of Museums and History of the Department of Tourism and Cultural
3 Affairs. (NRS 381.004) Section 1 of this bill revises the name of one such museum
4 by changing the name of the Nevada State Museum to the Nevada State Museum
5 Carson City. Sections 4-11 of this bill make conforming changes to reflect the
6 change in name.
7 Existing law creates the Board of Museums and History and prescribes certain
8 powers and duties of the Board relating to the functions of the Division. (NRS
9 381.002, 381.0045) Section 2 of this bill requires the Board to establish policies
10 and charges for certain services provided by the Division and its institutions.
11 Existing law requires the Administrator of the Division to authorize or require a
12 museum director to: (1) establish certain programs deemed proper and necessary by
13 the Administrator and the Board; and (2) house, preserve, care for and display or
14 exhibit property received by an institution administered by the museum director.
15 (NRS 381.0063) Section 3 of this bill removes the authority of the Board to make
16 determinations regarding such programs and eliminates the requirement that actions
17 related to property received by an institution be subject to the sole discretion of the
18 Board.

EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to museums; changing the name of the Nevada
State Museum to the Nevada State Museum Carson City;
revising the powers and duties of the Board of Museums
and History; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law establishes certain museums and historical societies as institutions
2 of the Division of Museums and History of the Department of Tourism and Cultural
3 Affairs. (NRS 381.004) Section 1 of this bill revises the name of one such museum
4 by changing the name of the Nevada State Museum to the Nevada State Museum
5 Carson City. Sections 4-11 of this bill make conforming changes to reflect the
6 change in name.
7 Existing law creates the Board of Museums and History and prescribes certain
8 powers and duties of the Board relating to the functions of the Division. (NRS
9 381.002, 381.0045) Section 2 of this bill requires the Board to establish policies
10 and charges for certain services provided by the Division and its institutions.
11 Existing law requires the Administrator of the Division to authorize or require a
12 museum director to: (1) establish certain programs deemed proper and necessary by
13 the Administrator and the Board; and (2) house, preserve, care for and display or
14 exhibit property received by an institution administered by the museum director.
15 (NRS 381.0063) Section 3 of this bill removes the authority of the Board to make
16 determinations regarding such programs and eliminates the requirement that actions
17 related to property received by an institution be subject to the sole discretion of the
18 Board.
