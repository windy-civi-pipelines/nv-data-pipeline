ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 409–ASSEMBLYMEMBERS HAFEN,
D’SILVA, GRAY; BROWN-MAY AND TORRES-FOSSETT
MARCH 12, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Growth and Infrastructure
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to special license plates.
(BDR 43-1058)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to motor vehicles; requiring the Department of
Motor Vehicles, under certain circumstances, to issue a
special license plate honoring certain veterans;
authorizing the combination of personalized prestige
license plates and certain special license plates associated
with military service; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires the Department of Motor Vehicles to issue personalized
2 prestige license plates and certain special license plates associated with military or
3 public service. (NRS 482.3667, 482.3757-482.3787)
4 Existing law requires the Department to issue special license plates for the
5 support of outreach programs and services for veterans and their families to: (1) a
6 veteran of the Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps or Coast Guard of the United
7 States, a reserve component thereof or the National Guard; (2) a female veteran; or
8 (3) the spouse, parent or child of a veteran or female veteran. Existing law requires
9 the plates to be inscribed with the seal of the branch of the Armed Forces, the seal
10 of the National Guard or an image representative of female veterans, as applicable.
11 (NRS 482.3763) Section 1 of this bill requires the issuance, upon application, of
12 such special license plates to veterans of the Space Force and the spouse, parent or
13 child of such veterans.
14 Existing law requires the Department to issue special license plates honoring
15 veterans of the Armed Forces of the United States who have been awarded the: (1)
16 Silver Star; or (2) Bronze Star Medal with “V” device, Combat V or Combat
17 Distinguishing Device. (NRS 482.3783) Section 1.5 of this bill requires the
18 Department to issue special license plates honoring veterans who have been
19 awarded the: (1) Air Force Cross; (2) Coast Guard Cross; (3) Distinguished Service
20 Cross; or (4) Navy Cross.
-
*AB409_R1*
– 2 –
21 Existing law requires the Department to issue, upon application, a special
22 license plate to each family member of a person: (1) killed in the line of duty while
23 on active duty in the Armed Forces of the United States; or (2) who died as a result
24 of injuries sustained while on active duty in the Armed Forces of the United States.
25 (NRS 482.3785, 482.3787) Sections 2 and 3 of this bill authorize a person to
26 request that personalized prestige license plates be combined with such special
27 license plates.

2 follows:
3 482.3763 1. The Director shall order the preparation of

25 license plates in addition to the fees for the special license plates for
26 the support of outreach programs and services for veterans and their

35 set of plates issued to the same applicant. Special license plates
36 issued pursuant to this section may only be used on a private
37 passenger vehicle, a noncommercial truck or a motor home.
