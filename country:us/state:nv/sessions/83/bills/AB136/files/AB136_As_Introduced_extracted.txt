Section 1:
A.B. 136
PREFILED JANUARY 28, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Natural Resources
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to animals.
(BDR 50-168)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: May have Fiscal Impact.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to animals; requiring certain persons that display
animals to the public or allow interactions between
animals and members of the public to post a notice with
the contact information for the local animal control
authority; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law: (1) prohibits a person from engaging in cruelty to animals; and
2 (2) authorizes any person who knows or has reasonable cause to believe that an
3 animal has been subject to an act of cruelty to report that act to a peace officer, an
4 officer of a society for the prevention of cruelty to animals that is authorized to
5 make arrests or an animal control officer. (NRS 574.053, 574.100) This bill
6 requires the owner or operator of an animal shelter or any commercial
7 establishment engaged in the business of selling or displaying animals to the public
8 or allowing animals to interact with members of the public to post a notice near the
9 enclosure of the animal: (1) notifying the public that if a person believes that the
10 animal is in danger, is being treated poorly or has been subjected to an act of animal
11 cruelty, the person may contact the local animal control authority; and (2) providing
12 the telephone number for such authority.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 574.053 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 574.053 1. Any person who knows or has reasonable cause to
3 believe that an animal has been subjected to an act of cruelty in
4 violation of NRS 574.100 may report the act of cruelty to any:
-
*AB136*
– 2 –
1 (a) Peace officer;
2 (b) Officer of a society for the prevention of cruelty to animals
3 who is authorized to make arrests pursuant to NRS 574.040; or
4 (c) Animal control officer.
5 2. Any person, law enforcement agency, society for the
6 prevention of cruelty to animals or animal control agency that
7 willfully releases data or information concerning the identity of a
8 person who made a report pursuant to subsection 1, except for the
9 purposes of a criminal investigation or prosecution, is guilty of a
10 misdemeanor.
11 3. The owner or operator of an animal shelter or a
12 commercial establishment engaged in the business of selling or
13 displaying animals to the public or allowing animals to interact
14 with the public, including, without limitation, a breeder, retailer or
15 dealer, shall conspicuously post within close proximity to the
16 enclosure of the animal the following notice in bold type:
17
18 IF YOU KNOW OR HAVE REASON TO BELIEVE
19 THIS ANIMAL IS IN DANGER, IS BEING TREATED
20 POORLY OR HAS BEEN SUBJECTED TO AN ACT OF
21 CRUELTY IN VIOLATION OF NRS 574.100, YOU MAY
22 CONTACT [telephone number for the local animal control
23 authority].
24
25 4. As used in this section:
26 (a) “Animal control authority” means any entity designated by
27 the county or city to enforce local ordinances or laws of this State
28 relating to the control, shelter or welfare of animals. The term
29 includes, without limitation, an animal control agency, a law
30 enforcement agency or a society for the prevention of cruelty to
31 animals in which an officer is authorized to make arrests pursuant
32 to NRS 574.040.
33 (b) “Animal shelter” has the meaning ascribed to it in
34 NRS 574.240.
35 (c) “Breeder” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.245.
36 (d) “Dealer” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.260.
37 (e) “Enclosure” means a structure used to restrict the
38 immediate movement of an animal to a limited amount of space,
39 including, without limitation, a room, pen, run, cage,
40 compartment or hutch.
41 (f) “Retailer” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.320.
