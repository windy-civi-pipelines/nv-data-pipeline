Section 1:
SECOND REPRINT A.B. 263
ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 263–ASSEMBLYMEMBERS MOSCA; CARTER,
CONSIDINE, D’SILVA, EDGEWORTH, FLANAGAN, HUNT,
JACKSON, KASAMA AND NGUYEN
FEBRUARY 20, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to homelessness.
(BDR 20-96)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: May have Fiscal Impact.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to homelessness; requiring the board of county
commissioners of certain counties to notify the
Department of Transportation before conducting a clean-
up of an encampment of homeless persons immediately
adjacent to certain rights-of-way; authorizing the
Department to consult with a homeless outreach team and
participate in the clean-up of an encampment of homeless
persons; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Section 1 of this bill requires the board of county commissioners of each
2 county whose population is 700,000 or more (currently only Clark County) to
3 notify the Department of Transportation before conducting a clean-up of an
4 encampment of homeless persons immediately adjacent to a right-of-way that is
5 controlled by the Department. Section 3 of this bill authorizes the Department to:
6 (1) consult with a homeless outreach team; and (2) participate in the clean-up of an
7 encampment of homeless persons in a right-of-way.

– 2 –
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 244 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. The board of county commissioners of each county whose
4 population is 700,000 or more shall notify the Department of
5 Transportation before conducting a clean-up of an encampment
6 of homeless persons immediately adjacent to a right-of-way that is
7 controlled by the Department of Transportation.
8 2. As used in this section, “right-of-way” has the meaning
9 ascribed to it in NRS 408.55014.
10 Sec. 2. (Deleted by amendment.)
11 Sec. 3. Chapter 408 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
12 thereto a new section to read as follows:
13 1. The Department may:
14 (a) Consult with a homeless outreach team; and
15 (b) Participate in the clean-up of an encampment of homeless
16 persons in a right-of-way, as defined in NRS 408.55014.
17 2. As used in this section, “homeless outreach team” means a
18 homeless outreach team established or designated by a board of
19 county commissioners or its designee for the purposes of providing
20 outreach and clean-ups related to homeless persons.
