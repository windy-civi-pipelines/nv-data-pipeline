Section 1:
FIRST REPRINT A.B. 82
ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 82–ASSEMBLYMEMBERS D’SILVA;
CONSIDINE, DALIA, FLANAGAN, GONZÁLEZ, HUNT,
KARRIS, MILLER, MOORE, MOSCA, NADEEM, NGUYEN,
ORENTLICHER AND TORRES-FOSSETT
PREFILED DECEMBER 9, 2024
____________
JOINT SPONSORS: SENATORS FLORES AND NGUYEN
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Requires the Governor to proclaim certain days of
observance. (BDR 19-60)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to days of observance; requiring the Governor to
annually proclaim certain days to be “Diwali Day,” “Eid
al-Fitr Day,” “Vaisakhi Day” and “Vesak Day” in the
State of Nevada; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Under existing law, various days, weeks and months of observance are
2 recognized in this State. (NRS 236.018-236.095) This bill requires the Governor to
3 annually proclaim certain days to be “Diwali Day,” “Eid al-Fitr Day,” “Vaisakhi
4 Day” and “Vesak Day” in the State of Nevada.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 236 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto the provisions set forth as sections 2 to 5, inclusive, of this
3 act.
4 Sec. 2. 1. The Governor shall annually proclaim the
5 fifteenth day of the eighth month of the Hindu calendar to be
6 “Diwali Day” in the State of Nevada.
-
*AB82_R1*
– 2 –
1 2. The proclamation may call upon the news media,
2 educators, business and labor leaders and appropriate
3 governmental officers to bring to the attention of the residents of
4 Nevada the diversity and inclusion of the cultures that celebrate
5 Diwali in the State of Nevada and throughout the world.
6 Sec. 3. 1. The Governor shall annually proclaim the first
7 day of the tenth month of the Islamic calendar to be “Eid al-Fitr
8 Day” in the State of Nevada.
9 2. The proclamation may call upon the news media,
10 educators, business and labor leaders and appropriate
11 governmental officers to bring to the attention of the residents of
12 Nevada the diversity and inclusion of the cultures that celebrate
13 Eid al-Fitr in the State of Nevada and throughout the world.
14 Sec. 4. 1. The Governor shall annually proclaim the first
15 day of the month of Vaisakh of the Sikh calendar to be “Vaisakhi
16 Day” in the State of Nevada.
17 2. The proclamation may call upon the news media,
18 educators, business and labor leaders and appropriate
19 governmental officers to bring to the attention of the residents of
20 Nevada the diversity and inclusion of the cultures that celebrate
21 Vaisakhi in the State of Nevada and throughout the world.
22 Sec. 5. 1. The Governor shall annually proclaim the day of
23 the first full moon in the month of May to be “Vesak Day,” also
24 known as “Buddha Day,” in the State of Nevada.
25 2. The proclamation may call upon the news media,
26 educators, business and labor leaders and appropriate
27 governmental officers to bring to the attention of the residents of
28 Nevada the diversity and inclusion of the cultures that celebrate
29 Vesak in the State of Nevada and throughout the world.
30 Sec. 6. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
