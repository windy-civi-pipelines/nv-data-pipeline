EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to education; [making an appropriation to the University of
Nevada, Las Vegas,] authorizing one or more institutions of higher
education within the Nevada System of Higher Education to
establish a pilot program to assess the competency of applicants for a
license to teach through the use of alternative assessment methods;
requiring certain regulations adopted by the Commission on
Professional Standards in Education to authorize an applicant for a
license to teach to be exempt from requirements to pass a competency
test in various subjects if the applicant completes the pilot program;
and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law creates the Commission on Professional Standards in Education and requires
2 the Commission to adopt regulations prescribing the qualifications for licensing teachers and
3 other educational personnel. (NRS 391.011, 391.019) Existing law additionally requires the
4 Commission to adopt regulations [: (1)] governing examinations for the initial licensing of
5 teachers and other educational personnel . [; and (2) authorizing an applicant for such licenses
6 to be exempt from the requirement to pass a competency test in basic reading, writing and
7 mathematics if he or she satisfies certain requirements] (NRS 391.021) Existing regulations
8 provide that an applicant is exempt from the requirement to pass [such a competency test] a
9 competency test in the principles and methods of teaching if the applicant submits to the
10 Department of Education evidence that he or she : (1) completed [: (1)] the [“Praxis Core
11 Academic Skills for Educators” in reading, mathematics and writing,] “Principles of
12 Learning and Teaching Test” prepared and administered by the Educational Testing
13 Service; or (2) [an equivalent test, as determined by the Department.] is applying for a
14 license with an endorsement in an area for which training in the principles and methods
15 of teaching is not required. (NAC 391.036) Section 1 of this bill provides that the
16 regulations adopted by the Commission must also authorize an applicant for an initial license
17 to teach to be exempt from the requirement to pass a competency test in [basic reading,
18 writing and mathematics] the principles and methods of teaching if he or she satisfactorily
19 completes the pilot program established pursuant to section 2 of this bill.

51 established by section 2 expires by limitation on July 1, 2029.
