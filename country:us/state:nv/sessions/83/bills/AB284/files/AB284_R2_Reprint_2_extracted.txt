ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 284–ASSEMBLYMEMBER BROWN-MAY
FEBRUARY 25, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Health and Human Services
SUMMARY—Requires the Director of the Department of Health
and Human Services to seek to establish certain rates
of reimbursement under Medicaid for certain devices
to treat epilepsy. (BDR S-571)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to Medicaid; requiring the Director of the
Department of Health and Human Services to seek to
establish certain rates of reimbursement under Medicaid
for vagus nerve stimulation therapy devices, replacement
parts for such a device and the procedures necessary to
implant, repair or replace a part of such a device; making
an appropriation; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires the Department of Health and Human Services to
2 administer the Medicaid program. (NRS 422.270) Section 2 of this bill requires the
3 Director of the Department to submit to the United States Secretary of Health and
4 Human Services a request to include under Medicaid to provide rates of
5 reimbursement greater than or equal to 82 percent of the cost for: (1) a vagus nerve
6 stimulation therapy device prescribed for the treatment of epilepsy; (2) any
7 replacement part for such a device; and (3) the procedure necessary to implant,
8 repair or replace a part of such a device. Section 2 also clarifies that the rates of
9 reimbursement established for the cost of a vagus nerve stimulation therapy device
10 and any replacement part for such a device must be separate from and in addition to
11 the rates of reimbursement for medical care necessary for the implantation or repair
12 of such a device or the replacement of a part for the device. Section 1 of this bill
13 makes an appropriation to and authorizes expenditures by the Division of Health
14 Care Financing and Policy of the Department for certain costs associated with
15 making the increases to rates of reimbursement under Medicaid required by
16 section 2.

THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Division of Health Care Financing and Policy
3 of the Department of Health and Human Services for carrying out
4 the provisions of section 2 of this act and making upgrades to
5 computer systems necessary to carry out those provisions the
6 following sums:
7 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................... $54,728
8 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 .................................. $240,004
9 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1
10 remaining at the end of the respective fiscal years must not be
11 committed for expenditure after June 30 of the respective fiscal
12 years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
13 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
14 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
15 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
16 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively, by
17 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
18 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and
19 must be reverted to the State General Fund on or before
20 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively.
21 3. Expenditure of the following sums not appropriated from the
22 State General Fund or the State Highway Fund is hereby authorized
23 by the Division of Health Care Financing and Policy of the
24 Department of Health and Human Services for the same purposes as
25 set forth in subsection 1:
26 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................. $188,270
27 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 .................................. $700,098
28 Sec. 2. 1. On or before October 1, 2025, the Director of the
29 Department of Health and Human Services shall submit to the
30 United States Secretary of Health and Human Services a request to
31 include under Medicaid:
32 (a) A rate of reimbursement for a vagus nerve stimulation
33 therapy device prescribed for the treatment of epilepsy that is
34 greater than or equal to 82 percent of the acquisition cost of such a
35 device;
36 (b) A rate of reimbursement for any replacement part for a vagus
37 nerve stimulation therapy device prescribed for the treatment of
38 epilepsy that is greater than or equal to 82 percent of the acquisition
39 cost of such a replacement part; and
40 (c) A rate of reimbursement for the procedure necessary to
41 implant, repair or replace a part of a vagus nerve stimulation therapy
-

1 device prescribed for the treatment of epilepsy that is greater than or
2 equal to 82 percent of the cost of the procedure.
3 2. The rates of reimbursement established pursuant to
4 paragraphs (a) and (b) of subsection 1 must be separate from and in
5 addition to the rates of reimbursement for the medical care
6 necessary for implantation or repair of a vagus nerve stimulation
7 therapy device or the replacement of a part for such a device.
8 3. The request submitted pursuant to subsection 1 must be

10 accepted by the Secretary.
11 Sec. 3. 1. This section and section 1 of this act become
12 effective on July 1, 2025.
