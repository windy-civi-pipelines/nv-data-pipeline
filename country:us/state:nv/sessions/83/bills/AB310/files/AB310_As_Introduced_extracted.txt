Section 1:
A.B. 310
MOORE, GONZÁLEZ; AND D’SILVA
FEBRUARY 27, 2025
____________
JOINT SPONSORS: SENATORS FLORES AND DOÑATE
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to The Immigrant Home
Foundation to provide mental health services and
legal assistance to victims of domestic violence.
(BDR S-1000)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to The Immigrant Home
Foundation to provide mental health services and legal
assistance to victims of domestic violence; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to The Immigrant Home Foundation for providing
3 mental health services and legal assistance to victims of domestic
4 violence the following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................. $250,000
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 .................................. $250,000
7 2. Upon acceptance of the money appropriated by subsection 1,
8 The Immigrant Home Foundation agrees to:
9 (a) Prepare and transmit a report to the Interim Finance
10 Committee on or before December 18, 2026, that describes each
11 expenditure made from the money appropriated by subsection 1
-
*AB310*
– 2 –
1 from the date on which the money was received by The Immigrant
2 Home Foundation through December 1, 2026;
3 (b) Prepare and transmit a final report to the Interim Finance
4 Committee on or before September 17, 2027, that describes each
5 expenditure made from the money appropriated by subsection 1
6 from the date on which the money was received by The Immigrant
7 Home Foundation through June 30, 2027; and
8 (c) Upon request of the Legislative Commission, make available
9 to the Legislative Auditor any of the books, accounts, claims,
10 reports, vouchers or other records of information, confidential or
11 otherwise, of The Immigrant Home Foundation, regardless of their
12 form or location, that the Legislative Auditor deems necessary to
13 conduct an audit of the use of the money appropriated by
14 subsection 1.
15 3. The sums appropriated by subsection 1 are available for
16 either fiscal year. Any remaining balance of those sums must not be
17 committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to
18 which the appropriation is made or any entity to which money from
19 the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner,
20 and any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be
21 spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity
22 to which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the
23 money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be
24 reverted to the State General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
25 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
H
