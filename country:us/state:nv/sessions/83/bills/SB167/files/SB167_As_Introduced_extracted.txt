Section 1:
S.B. 167
PREFILED FEBRUARY 3, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Commerce and Labor
SUMMARY—Prohibits the importation and sale of household
cleaning products for which animal testing was
performed. (BDR 52-892)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: Increases or Newly
Provides for Term of Imprisonment in County or City
Jail or Detention Facility.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to trade practices; prohibiting the importation and
sale of household cleaning products for which animal
testing was performed; providing penalties; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 This bill prohibits, under certain circumstances, a manufacturer from importing,
2 selling or offering for sale in this State any household cleaning product for which
3 animal testing was performed on or after January 1, 2030. This bill provides
4 exceptions from that prohibition for animal testing that is performed to comply with
5 a requirement of a regulatory agency of a foreign jurisdiction or, under certain
6 circumstances, a requirement of a federal or state regulatory agency. This bill
7 additionally provides that the prohibition does not apply to: (1) a household
8 cleaning product or ingredient in a household cleaning product if animal testing
9 was conducted for the household cleaning product or ingredient before January 1,
10 2030; or (2) a manufacturer that reviews, assesses or retains data obtained from
11 animal testing.
12 Existing law defines various activities that constitute deceptive trade practices
13 and provides for the imposition of civil and criminal penalties against persons who
14 engage in deceptive trade practices. (Chapter 598 of NRS) This bill makes it a
15 deceptive practice to violate the provisions of this bill.

*SB167*
– 2 –
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 598 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. Except as otherwise provided in this section, a
4 manufacturer shall not import for profit, sell or offer for sale in
5 this State any household cleaning product for which the
6 manufacturer knew or reasonably should have known that animal
7 testing was conducted or contracted by or on behalf of the
8 manufacturer or any supplier of the manufacturer if the animal
9 testing was conducted on or after January 1, 2030.
10 2. The prohibition in subsection 1 does not apply to animal
11 testing that is conducted:
12 (a) To comply with a requirement of a federal or state
13 regulatory agency if:
14 (1) The household cleaning product or ingredient in the
15 household cleaning product which is tested is in wide use and
16 cannot be replaced by another ingredient which is capable of
17 performing a similar function;
18 (2) A specific human health problem relating to the
19 household cleaning product or ingredient is substantiated and the
20 need to conduct animal testing is justified and supported by a
21 detailed protocol for research that is proposed as the basis for the
22 evaluation of the household cleaning product or ingredient; and
23 (3) There does not exist a method of testing other than
24 animal testing that is accepted for the relevant purpose by the
25 federal or state regulatory agency; or
26 (b) To comply with a requirement of a regulatory agency of a
27 foreign jurisdiction.
28 3. The provisions of this section do not apply to:
29 (a) A household cleaning product or ingredient in a household
30 cleaning product if animal testing was conducted for the
31 household cleaning product or ingredient before January 1, 2030,
32 even if the household cleaning product in its final form or
33 ingredient was manufactured on or after that date; or
34 (b) A manufacturer that reviews, assesses or retains data
35 obtained from animal testing.
36 4. A violation of this section constitutes a deceptive trade
37 practice for the purposes of NRS 598.0903 to 598.0999, inclusive.
38 5. As used in this section:
39 (a) “Animal testing” means any process or procedure that uses
40 a live, nonhuman vertebrate or cephalopod to obtain information
41 on the characteristics of a household cleaning product or any
42 ingredient thereof and which generates information regarding the
-
*SB167*
– 3 –
1 ability of a household cleaning product or ingredient to produce a
2 specific biological effect under specified conditions.
3 (b) “Household cleaning product” means any product
4 manufactured from chemicals that is designed to clean or provide
5 protection to household surfaces or furniture, including, without
6 limitation, task-specific cleaning products, such as spot removers,
7 stain removers, upholstery cleaners, glass cleaners, dish soap and
8 laundry detergent.
9 (c) “Ingredient” means any single chemical entity or mixture
10 used as a component in the manufacture of a household cleaning
11 product.
12 (d) “Manufacturer” means a person who imports, produces or
13 manufactures a household cleaning product and whose name
14 appears on the label of the household cleaning product.
15 (e) “Supplier” means any entity that supplies, directly or
16 through a third party, any ingredient used by a manufacturer in
17 the formulation of a household cleaning product.
18 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on January 1, 2030.
