Section 1:
A.B. 327
NGUYEN; AND ANDERSON
MARCH 3, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Legislative Operations and Elections
SUMMARY—Requires the Legislative Auditor to conduct an audit
of certain financial information and employment
matters relating to public broadcasting.
(BDR S-860)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to public broadcasting; requiring the Legislative
Auditor to conduct an audit of certain financial
information and employment matters associated with
Vegas PBS; requiring the Legislative Auditor to conduct
an audit of the Department of Education relating to grants
to public broadcasting organizations; and providing other
matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law establishes the Legislative Counsel Bureau, which consists of a
2 Legislative Commission, an Interim Finance Committee, a Director, an Audit
3 Division, a Fiscal Analysis Division, a Legal Division, a Research Division and an
4 Administrative Division. (NRS 218F.100) Existing law requires the Legislative
5 Auditor, who is the chief of the Audit Division, to conduct certain postaudits of
6 accounts, funds and other records of departments and boards, among other certain
7 entities, of the State to determine certain information. (NRS 218F.100, 218G.200)
8 This bill requires the Legislative Auditor to conduct an audit of the: (1) financial
9 management and employment practices of Vegas PBS, including, without
10 limitation, a focus on the budget, hiring practices and any other relevant matters
11 deemed necessary by the Legislative Commission; and (2) grants of money from
12 the Department of Education to public broadcasting organizations, including,
13 without limitation, an analysis of the distribution of grants and the historical
14 increases of the grants to each public broadcasting organization and any other
15 relevant matters deemed necessary by the Legislative Commission.
-
*AB327*
– 2 –
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. The Legislative Auditor shall conduct an audit
2 of the financial management and employment practices of Vegas
3 PBS. The audit of Vegas PBS must include, without limitation, a
4 focus on the budget, hiring practices and any other relevant matters
5 deemed necessary by the Legislative Commission.
6 2. The Legislative Auditor shall conduct an audit of the
7 distribution of grants from the Department of Education to public
8 broadcasting organizations. The audit of the Department of
9 Education must include, without limitation, an analysis of the
10 distribution of grants and the historical increases of the grants to
11 each public broadcasting organization and any other relevant
12 matters deemed necessary by the Legislative Commission.
13 3. On or before January 31, 2027, the Legislative Auditor shall
14 present a final written report of the audit conducted pursuant to this
15 section to the Audit Subcommittee of the Legislative Commission
16 created by NRS 218E.240.
17 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
