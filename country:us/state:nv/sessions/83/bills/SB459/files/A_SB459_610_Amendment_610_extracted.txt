EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to gaming; revising provisions relating to the operation of a
gaming salon; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires the Nevada Gaming Commission, with the advice and assistance of
2 the Nevada Gaming Control Board, to adopt regulations governing the standards of operation
3 for a gaming salon, including the policies and procedures governing certain games which may
4 be offered in a gaming salon. Existing law further provides that such regulations: (1) must
5 require a gaming salon to include table games; (2) may authorize a gaming salon to include
6 slot machines; and (3) must require minimum wagers of $500 for slot machines in a gaming
7 salon. (NRS 463.4073) This bill authorizes the regulations governing the standards of
8 operation for a gaming salon to allow for table games, slot machines or other gambling games,
9 or any combination thereof. This bill also requires the regulations to provide that the
10 minimum wagers for slot machines in a gaming salon must be established by the licensee and
11 approved by the Board.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 463.4073 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 463.4073 The Commission shall, with the advice and assistance of the Board,
3 adopt regulations setting forth:
4 1. The policies and procedures for approval of a license to operate a gaming
5 salon.
6 2. The standards of operation for a gaming salon, including, without
7 limitation, policies and procedures governing:
8 (a) Surveillance and security systems.
9 (b) The games offered. The regulations must provide that the games offered
10 [must] may include table games , [and may include] slot machines [.] or any other
11 gambling games, or any combination thereof.
Senate Amendment No. 610 to Senate Bill No. 459 Page 4
1 (c) Minimum wagers for any game offered. The regulations must provide that
2 minimum wagers for slot machines must [not be less than $500.] be established by
3 the licensee and approved by the Board.
4 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
