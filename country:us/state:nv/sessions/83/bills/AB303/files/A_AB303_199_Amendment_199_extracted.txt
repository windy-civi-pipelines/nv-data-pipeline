SUMMARY—[Requires certain projects to comply with] Revises provisions
governing public works. (BDR 28-866)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: May have Fiscal Impact.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to public works; requiring [certain] a contractor or
subcontractor engaged on certain public works that are projects for
the development , deployment or improvement of broadband,
telecommunication or fiber optic services [to comply with provisions
governing public works; requiring a contractor or subcontractor
engaged on such a project] to submit certain records to the Board of
Directors of the Nevada State Infrastructure Bank; specifying that the
provisions governing public works do not apply to certain
contracts; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law sets forth various requirements for public works, including, without
2 limitation, requirements for: (1) procedures for the awarding of contracts by a public body; (2)
3 the prevailing wage to be paid to certain workers employed in connection with the public
4 work; (3) the retention of records relating to workers employed in connection with the public
5 work and the wages and benefits paid to such workers; and (4) a contractor on a public work
6 to establish certain fair employment practices. (Chapter 338 of NRS)
7 [This] Section 2 of this bill provides that the provisions of existing law governing public
8 works do not apply to any [project] contract awarded for the development , deployment or
9 improvement of privately owned broadband, telecommunication or fiber optic services . [for
10 which public money has been provided and that is sponsored, financed, overseen or
11 administered by a public body in the same manner as if the public body awarded a contract for
12 the project, even if the project does not qualify as a public work.]
13 Existing law requires a contractor or subcontractor engaged on a public work to maintain
14 certain records concerning workers employed by the contractor or subcontractor in connection
15 with the public work and the wages and benefits paid to such workers. Existing law requires a
16 contractor or subcontractor to ensure that a copy of each such record for each calendar month
17 is received by the public body awarding the contract not later than 15 days after the end of
18 each month. (NRS 338.070) [This] Section 1 of this bill requires a contractor or subcontractor
19 engaged on a public work that is a project [described above] for the development,
Assembly Amendment No. 199 to Assembly Bill No. 303 Page 4
20 deployment or improvement of broadband, telecommunication or fiber optic services
21 who submits to [a] the public body awarding the contract a copy of such a record to
22 simultaneously submit a copy of the record to the Board of Directors of the Nevada State
23 Infrastructure Bank.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 338 of NRS is hereby amended by adding thereto a new
2 section to read as follows:
3 [1. In addition to any other requirements imposed by federal or state law,
4 the provisions of this chapter apply to any] A contractor or subcontractor
5 engaged on a public work that is a project for the development , deployment or
6 improvement of broadband, telecommunication or fiber optic services [for which
7 public money has been provided and that is sponsored, financed, overseen or
8 administered by a public body, including, without limitation, through the
9 disbursement of money by the public body or the issuance by the public body of a
10 request for procurement, in the same manner as if the public body awarded a
11 contract for the project, even if the project does not qualify as a public work.
12 2. A contractor or subcontractor engaged on a project described in
13 subsection 1 that] who, pursuant to subsection 6 of NRS 338.070, submits to [a]
14 the public body awarding the contract a copy of a record required pursuant to
15 subsection 5 of NRS 338.070 shall simultaneously submit a copy of the record to
16 the Board of Directors of the Nevada State Infrastructure Bank.
17 [3. As used in this section, “public body” has the meaning ascribed to it in
18 NRS 338.010 and includes, without limitation, the Office of Science, Innovation
19 and Technology in the Office of the Governor.]
20 Sec. 2. NRS 338.011 is hereby amended to read as follows:
21 338.011 The requirements of this chapter do not apply to a contract:
22 1. Awarded in compliance with chapter 332 or 333 of NRS which is directly
23 related to the normal operation of the public body or the normal maintenance of its
24 property.
25 2. Awarded to meet an emergency which results from a natural or artificially
26 created disaster and which threatens the health, safety or welfare of the public. If
27 the public body or its authorized representative determines that an emergency
28 exists, a contract or contracts necessary to contend with the emergency may be let
29 without complying with the requirements of this chapter. If such emergency action
30 was taken by the authorized representative, the authorized representative shall
31 report the contract or contracts to the public body at the next regularly scheduled
32 meeting of the public body.
33 3. Awarded for the development, deployment or improvement of privately
34 owned broadband, telecommunication or fiber optic services.
