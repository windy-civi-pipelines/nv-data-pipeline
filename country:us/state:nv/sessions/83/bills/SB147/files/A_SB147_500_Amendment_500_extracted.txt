EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to regional planning; establishing the Advisory Committee on
Regional Planning for Economic Resiliency; creating the Southern
Nevada Regional Planning for Economic Resiliency Pilot Program;
making an appropriation; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 This bill establishes the Southern Nevada Regional Planning for Economic Resiliency
2 Pilot Program. Section 7 of this bill requires the Regional Transportation Commission of
3 Southern Nevada to establish the Advisory Committee on Regional Planning for Economic
4 Resiliency to assist the Commission in carrying out the provisions of sections 1-10 of this bill.
5 Section 8 creates the Program to: (1) evaluate the current regional planning process and the
6 existing and future priorities and needs in the region of Clark County, Nevada, with a focus on
7 infrastructure projects, transit services and economic development and redevelopment
8 projects; and (2) identify sources of federal, state and other money that will support the
9 continued infrastructure, transit and economic needs of the region. Section 9 requires the
10 [Advisory Committee] Commission to conduct the Program with a focus on certain
11 population centers in the region and to study and perform community outreach on issues
12 relating to regional planning for economic development and resiliency in the region.
13 Section 10 requires: (1) the [Advisory Committee] Commission to prepare a regional
14 infrastructure plan for economic growth and resiliency in the region and a written report
15 describing the activities, findings, conclusions and recommendations of the Advisory
16 Committee; and (2) the Commission to transmit a copy of the plan and report to the Director
17 of the Legislative Counsel Bureau for transmittal to the Joint Interim Standing Committee on
18 Government Affairs.
19 Sections 2-6 define certain terms relating to the Program. Section 1 applies these
20 definitions to the provisions of sections 1-10.
21 Section 11 of this bill makes an appropriation to the Commission to establish and
22 implement the Program and for a staff member or a contractor to assist in the Program and to
23 carry out the provisions of sections 1-10.

1 Section 1. As used in sections 1 to 10, inclusive, of this act, unless the

11 local government is authorized to acquire, improve, equip, maintain or operate,
12 which may consist of all kinds of personal and real property.
13 Sec. 6. “Region” means Clark County, Nevada.
14 Sec. 7. 1. The Commission shall establish the Advisory Committee on
15 Regional Planning for Economic Resiliency to assist the Commission in carrying
16 out the provisions of sections 1 to 10, inclusive, of this act.
17 2. The Commission shall appoint at least [five] nine members to the Advisory
18 Committee which must be comprised of various stakeholders in the region who
19 must have experience in regional planning, economic development or
20 redevelopment, workforce development, infrastructure development or transit
21 services.
22 3. The members of the Advisory Committee must include, without
23 limitation:
24 (a) One member from the regional development authority which has
25 entered into a contract with the Office of Economic Development to serve the
26 region; and
27 (b) At least one member each from the following local governments:
28 (1) The City of Las Vegas;
29 (2) The City of Henderson;
30 (3) The City of North Las Vegas;
31 (4) Boulder City;
32 (5) The City of Mesquite; and
33 (6) Clark County.
34 4. Each member of the Advisory Committee serves a term of 2 years.

36 among its members at the first meeting of the Advisory Committee.

38 quorum for the transaction of business, and a majority of those members present at
39 any meeting is sufficient for any official action taken by the Advisory Committee.

41 and is not entitled to receive a per diem allowance or travel expenses.
42 [7. The Commission shall provide the Advisory Committee with such
43 administrative and staff support as is necessary to assist the Advisory Committee in
44 carrying out its duties set forth in sections 1 to 10, inclusive, of this act.]
45 Sec. 8. 1. The Southern Nevada Regional Planning for Economic
46 Resiliency Pilot Program is hereby created as a pilot program to support and sustain
47 the economic resiliency of the region by:

50 (e) Complete a high-level evaluation of the benefits of infrastructure projects

52 priorities identified in the Comprehensive Economic Development Strategy for

18 projects, transit services, economic development and redevelopment projects and
19 the methods to obtain grants for such projects and services; and
20 (k) Identify any lessons learned regarding the regional planning process and
21 regional coordination and opportunities for future improvements.]
22 Sec. 10. On or before June 30, 2027:
23 1. The [Advisory Committee] Commission shall prepare:
24 (a) A regional infrastructure plan for economic growth and resiliency in the
25 region, which must identify, without limitation:
26 (1) Infrastructure projects and transit services that support access to
27 existing, planned and proposed economic development and redevelopment centers
28 and projects that [the Advisory Committee has determined] are regionally
29 significant; and
30 (2) Potential federal, state and other resources that may be available to
31 support the projects and services identified pursuant to subparagraph (1); and
32 (b) A written report describing the activities, findings, conclusions and
33 recommendations of the Advisory Committee.
34 2. The Commission shall submit a copy of the plan and report prepared
35 pursuant to subsection 1 to the Director of the Legislature Counsel Bureau for
36 transmittal to the Joint Interim Standing Committee on Government Affairs.
37 Sec. 11. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
38 Regional Transportation Commission of Southern Nevada the sum of $650,000 to
39 establish and implement the Southern Nevada Regional Planning for Economic
40 Resiliency Pilot Program created by section 8 of this act and for a staff member or
41 contractor to assist the Commission and the Advisory Committee on Regional
42 Planning for Economic Resiliency created by section 7 of this act in carrying out
43 the provisions of sections 1 to 10, inclusive, of this act.
44 2. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by subsection 1 must not
45 be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
46 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the appropriation is
47 granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
48 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
49 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the
50 money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State
51 General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
52 Sec. 12. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025, and expires by limitation
53 on June 30, 2027.
