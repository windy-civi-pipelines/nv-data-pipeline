Section 1:
A.B. 125
PREFILED JANUARY 27, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to public bodies.
(BDR 18-909)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: May have Fiscal Impact.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to governmental administration; requiring that the
location on the official website of the State for the posting
of notices by public bodies that is maintained by the
Department of Administration includes a place to display
certain information relating to meetings; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 The Open Meeting Law requires that written notice of all meetings of a public
2 body be given at least 3 working days before the meeting. (NRS 241.020) The
3 notice must be posted, among other certain locations, on the location on the official
4 website of the State that is maintained by the Department of Administration for the
5 posting of notices by public bodies. (NRS 232.2175) This bill requires that a
6 schedule of meetings of public bodies also be posted on the location on the official
7 website of the State that is maintained by the Department.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 232.2175 is hereby amended to read as
2 follows:
3 232.2175 1. The Department shall establish and maintain a
4 location on the official website of the State for the posting of notices
5 by public bodies as required pursuant to NRS 241.020. The location
6 must be identified on the official website of the State in a clear and
7 conspicuous manner.
-
*AB125*
– 2 –
1 2. The location established pursuant to subsection 1 must
2 include [a] :
3 (a) A place to post a schedule of meetings of public bodies that,
4 without limitation, provides notice to the public of a meeting of a
5 public body at least 3 working days before the meeting.
6 (b) A place for the posting of electronic links to the Internet
7 website or any electronic mail addresses, if available, of each public
8 body which has posted a notice pursuant to NRS 241.020, from
9 which a person may request the information and supporting
10 materials that a public body must provide to a requester pursuant to
11 NRS 241.020.
12 3. The Department shall provide for:
13 (a) The transmission to the Department by public bodies of:
14 (1) Notices required pursuant to NRS 241.020; [and]
15 (2) Schedules of meetings of public bodies; and
16 (3) The Internet website or any electronic mail addresses, if
17 available, of a public body that has submitted a notice for posting on
18 the official website of the State.
19 (b) The timely and efficient posting of such notices , schedules
20 and electronic links to addresses on the official website of Nevada.
21 4. The Department may adopt regulations to carry out the
22 provisions of this section.
