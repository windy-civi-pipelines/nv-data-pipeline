Section 1:
A.B. 342
HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
(ON BEHALF OF THE LEGISLATIVE COMMITTEE ON SENIOR
CITIZENS, VETERANS AND ADULTS WITH SPECIAL NEEDS)
MARCH 3, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Designates the month of March as “Women Veterans
History Month.” (BDR 19-459)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to periods of observance; designating the month
of March of each year as “Women Veterans History
Month” in this State; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Under existing law, various days, weeks and months of observance are
2 recognized in this State. (NRS 236.018-236.095) This bill designates the month of
3 March of each year as “Women Veterans History Month” in this State and requires
4 the Governor to issue annually a proclamation encouraging the observance of
5 Women Veterans History Month.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 236 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. The month of March of each year is designated as
4 “Women Veterans History Month” in this State.
5 2. The Governor shall issue annually a proclamation
6 encouraging the observance of Women Veterans History Month.
7 The proclamation must, without limitation, call upon the news
8 media, educators and appropriate governmental officers to bring
-
*AB342*
– 2 –
1 to the attention of the residents of Nevada the history of women
2 veterans and the contributions of women veterans to the State of
3 Nevada and the United States.
4 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
