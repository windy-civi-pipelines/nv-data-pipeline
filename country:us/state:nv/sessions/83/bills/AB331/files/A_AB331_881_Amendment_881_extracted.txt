ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 331–ASSEMBLYMEMBER BROWN-MAY
MARCH 3, 2025
_______________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to the Division of Public and Behavioral
Health of the Department of Health and Human Services for a
grant program to expand the biennial survey administered
pursuant to the Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System
developed by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention of
the United States Department of Health and Human Services.
(BDR S-1044)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Division of Public and Behavioral
Health of the Department of Health and Human Services for a grant
program to expand the biennial survey administered pursuant to the
Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System developed by the Centers
for Disease Control and Prevention of the United States Department of
Health and Human Services; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 the Division of Public and Behavioral Health of the Department of Health and
3 Human Services for a grant program to expand the biennial survey administered
4 pursuant to the Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System developed by the Centers
5 for Disease Control and Prevention of the United States Department of Health and
6 Human Services the following sums:
7 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ........................................................ $220,000
8 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ........................................................ $220,000
9 2. The Division of Public and Behavioral Health of the Department of
10 Health and Human Services shall develop and implement a grant program to award
11 grants from the money appropriated by subsection 1 to institutions within the
12 Nevada System of Higher Education [, boards of trustees of school districts or
13 governing bodies of charter schools] to expand the biennial survey administered
14 pursuant to the Youth Risk Behavior Surveillance System developed by the Centers
Assembly Amendment No. 881 to Assembly Bill No. 331 Page 4
1 for Disease Control and Prevention of the United States Department of Health and
2 Human Services as required by NRS 392.422.
3 3. The sums appropriated by subsection 1 are available for either fiscal year.
4 Any remaining balance of those sums must not be committed for expenditure after
5 June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to
6 which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any
7 manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be spent for
8 any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was
9 appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently granted or
10 transferred, and must be reverted to the State General Fund on or before September
11 17, 2027.
12 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
