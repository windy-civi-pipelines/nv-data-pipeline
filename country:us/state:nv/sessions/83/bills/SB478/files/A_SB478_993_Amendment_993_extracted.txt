SENATE BILL NO. 478–COMMITTEE ON FINANCE
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 15, 2025
_______________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations [to the Supreme Court of Nevada] and
requires the issuance of bonds for costs associated with the
[replacement of certain equipment and the upgrading of a certain
information system.] Judicial Department of State
Government. (BDR S-1187)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations [to the Supreme Court of Nevada] and requiring
the issuance of certain bonds for costs associated with the
[replacement of certain equipment and the upgrading of a certain
information system;] Judicial Department of State Government; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 the Supreme Court of Nevada the sum of $814,720 for the Administrative Office of
3 the Courts budget account for the costs of upgrading the multicounty integrated
4 justice information system.
5 2. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by subsection 1 must not
6 be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
7 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the appropriation is
8 granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
9 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
10 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the
11 money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State
12 General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
13 Sec. 2. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
14 Supreme Court of Nevada the sum of $1,413,605 for the Administrative Office of
15 the Courts budget account for the replacement of audiovisual equipment.

21 respectively, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
22 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted
23 to the State General Fund on or before September 18, 2026, and September 17,

25 Sec. 3.5. 1. The State Board of Finance shall issue general obligation
26 bonds of the State of Nevada in the face amount of not more than $9,000,000 in
27 the 2025-2027 biennium, the proceeds of which must be used to acquire the
28 real property described in subsection 3.
29 2. The State Land Registrar, on behalf of the Department of
30 Administration, may negotiate, enter into and execute an agreement with the
31 owner for the purchase and sale of the real property described in subsection 3.
32 3. The real property, with its improvements, that the State Land
33 Registrar is authorized to acquire pursuant to subsection 2 is commonly
34 known as the Nevada Supreme Court Building, located at 408 East Clark
