Section 1:
FIRST REPRINT S.B. 117
SENATE BILL NO. 117–SENATOR CRUZ-CRAWFORD
PREFILED JANUARY 27, 2025
____________
JOINT SPONSOR: ASSEMBLYMEMBER MOORE
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to services for veterans.
(BDR 37-795)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to veterans; requiring the Director of the
Department of Veterans Services to make available to
certain agencies, resource centers or programs any
existing informational materials concerning the process
for applying for an upgrade to the character of a veteran’s
discharge from service or a change in the narrative reason
for a veteran’s discharge from service; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires the Director of the Department of Veterans Services to
2 conduct outreach to, and provide assistance designed for the unique needs of,
3 veterans and members of the military who are lesbian, gay, bisexual or transgender
4 and their spouses and dependents. Existing law further requires this outreach and
5 assistance to include, without limitation, a requirement for the Director to provide
6 assistance in applying for an upgrade to the character of the veteran’s discharge
7 from service or a change in the narrative reason for the veteran’s discharge from
8 service. (NRS 417.121) This bill additionally requires the Director to, as part of
9 such outreach and assistance, make available to any agency, resource center or
10 program in this State which provides services and resources to veterans and
11 members of the military regarding the benefits, services, programs and assistance
12 available to veterans any existing informational materials concerning the process
13 for applying for an upgrade to the character of the veteran’s discharge from service
14 or a change in the narrative reason for the veteran’s discharge from service.

THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 417.121 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 417.121 1. The Director shall conduct outreach to, and
3 provide assistance designed for the unique needs of, veterans and
4 members of the military who are lesbian, gay, bisexual or
5 transgender and their spouses and dependents, including, without
6 limitation [, providing] :
7 (a) Providing assistance in applying for an upgrade to the
8 character of the veteran’s discharge from service or a change in the
9 narrative reason for the veteran’s discharge from service [.] ; and
10 (b) Making available to any agency, resource center or
11 program in this State which provides services and resources to
12 veterans and members of the military regarding the benefits,
13 services, programs and assistance available to veterans any
14 existing informational materials concerning the process for
15 applying for an upgrade to the character of the veteran’s
16 discharge from service or a change in the narrative reason for the
17 veteran’s discharge from service.
18 2. As used in this section, “veteran” means a resident of this
19 State who meets the qualifications of subsection 1 of NRS 417.005.
