EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to veterans; requiring the Director of the Department of Veterans
Services to [develop and] make available to certain agencies, resource
centers or programs [a program of training and] any existing
informational materials concerning the process for applying for an
upgrade to the character of a veteran’s discharge from service or a
change in the narrative reason for a veteran’s discharge from service;
and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires the Director of the Department of Veterans Services to conduct
2 outreach to, and provide assistance designed for the unique needs of, veterans and members of
3 the military who are lesbian, gay, bisexual or transgender and their spouses and dependents.
4 Existing law further requires this outreach and assistance to include, without limitation, a
5 requirement for the Director to provide assistance in applying for an upgrade to the character of
6 the veteran’s discharge from service or a change in the narrative reason for the veteran’s
7 discharge from service. (NRS 417.121) This bill additionally requires the Director to, as part of
8 such outreach and assistance, [develop and] make available to any agency, resource center or
9 program in this State which provides services and resources to veterans and members of the
10 military regarding the benefits, services, programs and assistance available to veterans [a
11 program of training and] any existing informational materials concerning the process for
12 applying for an upgrade to the character of the veteran’s discharge from service or a change in
13 the narrative reason for the veteran’s discharge from service.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 417.121 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 417.121 1. The Director shall conduct outreach to, and provide assistance
3 designed for the unique needs of, veterans and members of the military who are
Senate Amendment No. 499 to Senate Bill No. 117 Page 4
1 lesbian, gay, bisexual or transgender and their spouses and dependents, including,
2 without limitation [, providing] :
3 (a) Providing assistance in applying for an upgrade to the character of the
4 veteran’s discharge from service or a change in the narrative reason for the
5 veteran’s discharge from service [.] ; and
6 (b) [Developing and making] Making available to any agency, resource
7 center or program in this State which provides services and resources to veterans
8 and members of the military regarding the benefits, services, programs and
9 assistance available to veterans [a program of training and] any existing
10 informational materials concerning the process for applying for an upgrade to
11 the character of the veteran’s discharge from service or a change in the narrative
12 reason for the veteran’s discharge from service.
13 2. As used in this section, “veteran” means a resident of this State who meets
14 the qualifications of subsection 1 of NRS 417.005.
