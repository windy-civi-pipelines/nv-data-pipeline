A.B. 168
PREFILED JANUARY 31, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Growth and Infrastructure
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to bicycles, electric
bicycles and electric scooters. (BDR 43-598)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to public safety; revising provisions governing the
operation of a bicycle, an electric bicycle or an electric
scooter on a roadway; providing a civil penalty; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law grants certain rights to a person operating a bicycle, an electric
2 bicycle or an electric scooter upon a roadway and subjects any such person to
3 certain duties and responsibilities while operating the bicycle, electric bicycle or
4 electric scooter on a roadway. (NRS 484B.760-484B.785) Section 1 of this bill: (1)
5 requires a person operating a bicycle, an electric bicycle or an electric scooter to
6 stop in obedience to a stop sign or signal displaying a steady red light; and (2)
7 creates an exception to the requirement to stop in obedience to a stop sign if the
8 person can safely proceed through the intersection without stopping and certain
9 other requirements are met. Section 1 also authorizes a person operating a bicycle,
10 an electric bicycle or an electric scooter to proceed cautiously through an
11 intersection where the signal is a steady red signal or make certain turns if the
12 person stops before entering the intersection and certain other requirements are met.
13 Section 2 of this bill provides that a violation of the provisions of section 1 is a
14 civil infraction. Section 3 of this bill makes a confirming change to indicate the
15 applicability of the requirements prescribed by section 1.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 484B of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. Except as otherwise provided in subsection 2, a person
4 operating a bicycle, an electric bicycle or an electric scooter upon

2 signal displaying a steady red signal.

6 stopping if the person:
7 (a) Ascertains that such movement can be made safely;

21 (c) Yields the right-of-way to pedestrians and all vehicles
22 whose proximity constitutes an immediate hazard.
23 4. A person operating a bicycle, an electric bicycle or an
24 electric scooter upon a roadway shall not turn left through an
25 intersection where the signal is a steady red signal unless the
26 roadway onto which the person is turning is a one-way roadway.
27 Sec. 2. NRS 484B.760 is hereby amended to read as follows:
28 484B.760 1. It is a civil infraction punishable pursuant to
29 NRS 484A.703 to 484A.705, inclusive, for any person to do any act
30 forbidden or fail to perform any act required in NRS 484B.768 to
31 484B.790, inclusive [.] , and section 1 of this act.
32 2. The parent of any child and the guardian of any ward shall
33 not authorize or knowingly permit the child or ward to violate any
34 of the provisions of chapters 484A to 484E, inclusive, of NRS.
35 3. The provisions applicable to bicycles, electric bicycles and
36 electric scooters apply whenever a bicycle, an electric bicycle or an
37 electric scooter is operated upon any highway or upon any path set
38 aside for the exclusive use of bicycles, electric bicycles and electric
39 scooters subject to those exceptions stated herein.

43 to all of the duties applicable to the driver of a vehicle except as
44 otherwise provided in NRS 484B.767 to 484B.790, inclusive, and
