Section 1:
S.B. 232
STONE, DOÑATE; DALY AND HANSEN
FEBRUARY 24, 2025
____________
JOINT SPONSORS: ASSEMBLYMEMBERS
NGUYEN, O’NEILL; AND D’SILVA
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation for the construction of a
veterans and community center in Virginia City,
Nevada. (BDR S-1055)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Evans-Kendall Veterans of
Foreign Wars Post 8071 for the construction of a veterans
and community center; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Evans-Kendall Veterans of Foreign Wars Post
3 8071 the sum of $750,000 for the construction of a facility to
4 provide services to veterans and community members of Virginia
5 City, Nevada.
6 2. Upon acceptance of the money appropriated by subsection 1,
7 the Evans-Kendall Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 8071 agrees to:
8 (a) Prepare and transmit a report to the Interim Finance
9 Committee on or before December 18, 2026, that describes each
10 expenditure made from the money appropriated by subsection 1
11 from the date on which the money was received by the
-
*SB232*
– 2 –
1 Evans-Kendall Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 8071 through
2 December 1, 2026;
3 (b) Prepare and transmit a final report to the Interim Finance
4 Committee on or before September 17, 2027, that describes each
5 expenditure made from the money appropriated by subsection 1
6 from the date on which the money was received by the Evans-
7 Kendall Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 8071 through June 30, 2027;
8 and
9 (c) Upon request of the Legislative Commission, make available
10 to the Legislative Auditor any of the books, accounts, claims,
11 reports, vouchers or other records of information, confidential or
12 otherwise, of the Evans-Kendall Veterans of Foreign Wars Post
13 8071, regardless of their form or location, that the Legislative
14 Auditor deems necessary to conduct an audit of the use of the
15 money appropriated by subsection 1.
16 3. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by
17 subsection 1 must not be committed for expenditure after June 30,
18 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
19 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
20 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
21 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
22 September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was
23 appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently
24 granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State General
25 Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
26 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
