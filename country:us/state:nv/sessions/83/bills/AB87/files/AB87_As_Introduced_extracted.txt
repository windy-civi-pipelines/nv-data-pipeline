Section 1:
A.B. 87
ON GROWTH AND INFRASTRUCTURE
(ON BEHALF OF THE JOINT INTERIM STANDING
COMMITTEE ON NATURAL RESOURCES)
PREFILED JANUARY 6, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Growth and Infrastructure
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation relating to wildlife
crossings. (BDR S-390)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Wildlife Crossings
Account for the design, construction and identification of
new wildlife crossings and other related highway features;
and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Wildlife Crossings Account created by NRS 408.656
3 the sum of $5,000,000 to design, construct and identify new wildlife
4 crossings and other related highway features to improve
5 permeability for wildlife in this State.
6 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
H
