Section 1:
EXEMPT
S.B. 489
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 15, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Department of
Corrections for the purchase and replacement of
certain equipment and furniture. (BDR S-1207)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Department of Corrections
for the purchase and replacement of certain equipment
and furniture at certain transitional housing facilities; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Northern
3 Nevada Transitional Housing budget account for the purchase of
4 small equipment the following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ...................................... $1,660
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ...................................... $1,660
7 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1
8 remaining at the end of the respective fiscal years must not be
9 committed for expenditure after June 30 of the respective fiscal
10 years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
11 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
12 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
13 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
14 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively, by
-
*SB489*
– 2 –
1 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
2 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred,
3 and must be reverted to the State General Fund on or before
4 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively.
5 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
6 Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Northern Nevada
7 Transitional Housing budget account the sum of $8,228 for the
8 replacement of maintenance equipment.
9 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
10 Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Casa Grande
11 Transitional Housing budget account the sum of $12,312 for the
12 replacement of sunshade canopies and office chairs.
13 Sec. 4. Any remaining balance of the appropriations made by
14 sections 2 and 3 of this act must not be committed for expenditure
15 after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made
16 or any entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or
17 otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the
18 appropriated money remaining must not be spent for any purpose
19 after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money
20 was appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently
21 granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State General
22 Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
23 Sec. 5. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
