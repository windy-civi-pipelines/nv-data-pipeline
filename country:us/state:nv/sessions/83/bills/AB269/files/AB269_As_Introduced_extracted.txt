Section 1:
A.B. 269
FEBRUARY 20, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to education.
(BDR 18-618)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to education; expanding the applicability of the
Student Loan Repayment for Providers of Health Care in
Underserved Communities Program; and providing other
matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law creates the Student Loan Repayment for Providers of Health Care
2 in Underserved Communities Program to repay the student education loans of
3 qualified providers of health care who work in certain underserved communities.
4 (NRS 226.458) Existing law defines “provider of health care” for purposes of the
5 Program. (NRS 226.454) Section 1 of this bill adds a licensed marriage and family
6 therapist, clinical alcohol and drug counselor and alcohol and drug counselor and a
7 certified problem gambling counselor to the definition of “provider of health care”
8 for such purposes, thereby expanding the applicability of the Program.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 226.454 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 226.454 “Provider of health care” means:
3 1. A physician;
4 2. A physician assistant licensed pursuant to chapter 630 or
5 633 of NRS;
6 3. A dentist;
7 4. A licensed nurse;
