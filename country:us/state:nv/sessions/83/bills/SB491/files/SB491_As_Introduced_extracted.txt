AN ACT making appropriations to the Training Division of the
Department of Public Safety for the replacement of
equipment, computer hardware and associated software,
beds and mattresses and for maintenance projects and
services; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN

2 General Fund to the Training Division of the Department of Public
