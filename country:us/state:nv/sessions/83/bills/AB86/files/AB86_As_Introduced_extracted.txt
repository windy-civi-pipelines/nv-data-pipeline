Section 1:
A.B. 86
(ON BEHALF OF THE LEGISLATIVE COMMITTEE
FOR THE REVIEW AND OVERSIGHT OF THE
TAHOE REGIONAL PLANNING AGENCY AND
THE MARLETTE LAKE WATER SYSTEM)
PREFILED JANUARY 6, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Natural Resources
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to logging permits.
(BDR 47-377)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to forestry; revising provisions relating to logging
permits; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Under existing law, a timber owner or the agent of a timber owner is required
2 to secure a logging permit from the State Forester Firewarden prior to any logging
3 or cutting operation. (NRS 528.042) Existing law defines the term “logging
4 operation” as the commercial harvesting of trees by a timber owner or the agent of
5 a timber owner using certain methods. (NRS 528.0175) This bill removes the
6 requirement to secure a logging permit for a cutting operation.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 528.042 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 528.042 1. Prior to any logging [or cutting] operation, any
3 timber owner or the agent of the timber owner shall secure a logging
4 permit from the State Forester Firewarden.
5 2. Application for such permit shall be made to the nearest
6 office of the Division of Forestry of the State Department of
7 Conservation and Natural Resources.
-
*AB86*
– 2 –
1 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
H
