Raw Text:
Assembly Bill No. 445–Assemblymember Monroe-Moreno
CHAPTER..........
AN ACT relating to libraries; providing that library staff are
immune from criminal or civil liability for certain good faith
acts relating to providing access to or assisting pupils or
library patrons in accessing library materials; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
Existing law requires the board of trustees of a school district to purchase all
new library books and supplies to be used by pupils of the school district. (NRS
393.170) Existing law also provides for the creation of publicly supported libraries
and information centers to meet the informational needs of all citizens. (NRS
379.002) Sections 1 and 2 of this bill provide that the staff members of a school
library or public library are immune from criminal or civil liability for certain good
faith acts relating to providing access to or assisting pupils or library patrons in
accessing library materials.
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
Section 1. Chapter 388 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
thereto a new section to read as follows:
1. A school library staff member is immune from criminal or
civil liability for good faith acts relating to providing access to or
assisting pupils in accessing library materials of a school library.
2. As used in this section:
(a) “Library materials” includes, without limitation, books,
periodicals, pamphlets, journals, newspapers, audio materials,
audiovisual materials, instructional materials, maps, databases,
government documents, records and photographs.
(b) “School library staff member” means a school library
media specialist, school librarian, any licensed or unlicensed
employee of a school assigned to duties in a school library or any
person carrying out or assisting with the functions of a school
library media specialist or school librarian.
Sec. 2. Chapter 379 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
thereto a new section to read as follows:
1. Any staff member of a public library, including, without
limitation, a librarian employed by a public library, is immune
from criminal or civil liability for good faith acts relating to
providing access to or assisting library patrons in accessing library
materials of the public library.

Sec. 3. (Deleted by amendment.)
