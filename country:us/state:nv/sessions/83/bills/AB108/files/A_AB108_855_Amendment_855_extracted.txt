EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Outdoor Education and Recreation Grant
Program Account for carrying out the Outdoor Education and
Recreation Grant Program; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
2 Outdoor Education and Recreation Grant Program Account created by NRS
3 407A.615 for carrying out the Outdoor Education and Recreation Grant Program
4 created by NRS 407A.605 the following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ..................................... [$500,000] $250,000
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ..................................... [$500,000] $250,000
7 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
