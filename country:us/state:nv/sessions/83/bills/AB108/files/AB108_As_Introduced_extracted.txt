Section 1:
A.B. 108
JAUREGUI AND YEAGER
PREFILED JANUARY 21, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to the Outdoor Education
and Recreation Grant Program Account.
(BDR S-875)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Outdoor Education and
Recreation Grant Program Account for carrying out the
Outdoor Education and Recreation Grant Program; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Outdoor Education and Recreation Grant Program
3 Account created by NRS 407A.615 for carrying out the Outdoor
4 Education and Recreation Grant Program created by NRS 407A.605
5 the following sums:
6 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................. $500,000
7 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 .................................. $500,000
8 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
H
