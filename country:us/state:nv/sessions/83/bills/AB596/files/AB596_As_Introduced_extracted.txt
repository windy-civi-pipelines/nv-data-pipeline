AN ACT relating to public employees; making appropriations for
the implementation of certain collective bargaining
agreements; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor
3 the sum of $90,209,716 for the costs of implementing the provisions
4 of the collective bargaining agreement covering personnel
5 represented by the bargaining units established in paragraphs (a), (e)
6 and (f) of subsection 1 of NRS 288.515.
7 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
8 the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor the sum of
9 $57,464,302 for the costs of implementing the provisions of the
10 collective bargaining agreement covering personnel represented by
11 the bargaining unit established in paragraph (c) of subsection 1 of
12 NRS 288.515.
13 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
14 the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor the sum of

30 the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor the sum of
31 $65,100 for the costs of implementing the provisions of the
32 collective bargaining agreement covering personnel represented by
33 the bargaining unit established in paragraph (o) of subsection 1 of
34 NRS 288.515.
35 9. Any remaining balance of the appropriations made by this
36 section must not be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027,
37 by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to
38 which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
39 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
40 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
41 September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was
42 appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently
43 granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State General
44 Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
