Section 1:
S.B. 133
PREFILED JANUARY 30, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to the Nevada Center for
Civic Engagement to support civics education
programs. (BDR S-104)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Nevada Center for Civic
Engagement to support civics education programs in
elementary, junior high, middle and high schools in this
State and to expand civics education programs for adults;
and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Nevada Center for Civic Engagement the sum
3 of $800,000 to support civics education programs, including,
4 without limitation, National History Day, Civics in Action Summit,
5 KidsVention and We the People: The Citizen and the Constitution
6 Program, in Nevada’s elementary, junior high, middle and high
7 schools and to expand civics education programs for adults.
8 2. Of the sum appropriated by subsection 1, the Nevada Center
9 for Civic Engagement shall not expend more than $400,000 in
10 Fiscal Year 2025-2026.
11 3. Upon acceptance of the money appropriated by subsection 1,
12 the Nevada Center for Civic Engagement agrees to:
13 (a) Prepare and transmit a report to the Interim Finance
14 Committee on or before December 18, 2026, that describes each
15 expenditure made from the money appropriated by subsection 1
-
*SB133*
– 2 –
1 from the date on which the money was received by the Nevada
2 Center for Civic Engagement through December 1, 2026;
3 (b) Prepare and transmit a final report to the Interim Finance
4 Committee on or before September 17, 2027, that describes each
5 expenditure made from the money appropriated by subsection 1
6 from the date on which the money was received by the Nevada
7 Center for Civic Engagement through June 30, 2027; and
8 (c) Upon request of the Legislative Commission, make available
9 to the Legislative Auditor any of the books, accounts, claims,
10 reports, vouchers or other records of information, confidential or
11 otherwise, of the Nevada Center for Civic Engagement, regardless
12 of their form or location, that the Legislative Auditor deems
13 necessary to conduct an audit of the use of the money appropriated
14 pursuant to subsection 1.
15 4. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by
16 subsection 1 must not be committed for expenditure after June 30,
17 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
18 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
19 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
20 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
21 September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was
22 appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently
23 granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State General
24 Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
25 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
