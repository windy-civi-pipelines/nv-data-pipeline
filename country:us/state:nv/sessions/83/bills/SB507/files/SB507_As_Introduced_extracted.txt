Section 1:
EXEMPT
S.B. 507
MAY 28, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to the Office of Nevada
Boards, Commissions and Councils Standards of the
Department of Business and Industry.
(BDR 18-1252)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to governmental administration; authorizing the
Office of Nevada Boards, Commissions and Councils
Standards of the Department of Business and Industry to
adopt regulations prescribing a fee for any service
provided by the Office to a professional or occupational
licensing board under the purview of the Office; removing
the Commission on Postsecondary Education of the
Employment Security Division of the Department of
Employment, Training and Rehabilitation from the
purview of the Office; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law creates the Office of Nevada Boards, Commissions and Councils
2 Standards of the Department of Business and Industry and sets forth the powers and
3 duties of the Office. (NRS 232.8413, 232.8415) Existing law provides, with certain
4 exceptions, that all professional and occupational licensing boards created by the
5 Legislature are under the purview of the Office. (NRS 232.8415) Section 8 of this
6 bill removes the Commission on Postsecondary Education of the Employment
7 Security Division of the Department of Employment, Training and Rehabilitation
8 from the purview of the Office.
9 Section 5 of this bill authorizes the Office to: (1) prescribe a fee to cover the
10 costs incurred by the Office for any service provided by the Office to a professional
11 or occupational licensing board under the purview of the Office; and (2) adopt such
12 procedures as the Office may deem appropriate for the billing or collection of such
13 fees from a professional or occupational licensing board to which such a service is
14 provided. Section 5 requires all money collected by the Office from such fees to be
-
*SB507*
– 2 –
15 deposited in the State Treasury for credit to the Office of Nevada Boards,
16 Commissions and Councils Standards Account, which is created by section 6 of
17 this bill.
18 Sections 3 and 4 of this bill define certain terms for the purposes of the
19 provisions of this bill and the provisions of existing law governing the Office.
20 Section 2 of this bill makes the definitions in sections 3 and 4 apply to the
21 provisions of this bill and the provisions of existing law governing the Office.
22 Section 7 of this bill makes a conforming change to apply the definitions applicable
23 to existing provisions governing the Department of Business and Industry to the
24 provisions of sections 2-6.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 232 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto the provisions set forth as sections 2 to 6, inclusive, of this
3 act.
4 Sec. 2. As used in NRS 232.8413 and 232.8415 and sections
5 2 to 6, inclusive, of this act, unless the context otherwise requires,
6 the words and terms defined in sections 3 and 4 of this act have
7 the meanings ascribed to them in those sections.
8 Sec. 3. “Deputy Director” means the Deputy Director of the
9 Office.
10 Sec. 4. “Office” means the Office of Nevada Boards,
11 Commissions and Councils Standards of the Department created
12 by NRS 232.8413.
13 Sec. 5. 1. The Office may, by regulation:
14 (a) Prescribe a fee to cover the costs incurred by the Office for
15 any service provided by the Office to a professional or
16 occupational licensing board under the purview of the Office
17 pursuant to NRS 232.8415; and
18 (b) Adopt such procedures as the Office may deem appropriate
19 for the billing or collection of such fees from a professional or
20 occupational licensing board to which such a service is provided.
21 2. Any money the Office receives pursuant to this section
22 must be deposited in the State Treasury for credit to the Account.
23 3. As used in this section, “Account” means the Office of
24 Nevada Boards, Commissions and Councils Standards Account
25 created by section 6 of this act.
26 Sec. 6. 1. The Office of Nevada Boards, Commissions and
27 Councils Standards Account is hereby created in the State General
28 Fund. The Account must be administered by the Deputy Director
29 and money in the Account may be expended only to carry out the
30 provisions of NRS 232.8413 and 232.8415 and sections 2 to 6,
31 inclusive, of this act.
-
*SB507*
– 3 –
1 2. The interest and income earned on the money in the
2 Account, after deducting applicable charges, must be credited to
3 the Account.
4 3. Any money in the Account and any unexpended
5 appropriations made to the Account from the State General Fund
6 remaining at the end of a fiscal year do not revert to the State
7 General Fund, and the balance in the Account must be carried
8 forward to the next fiscal year.
9 4. The Deputy Director may apply for and accept any gift,
10 grant, donation or appropriation for deposit in the Account.
11 Sec. 7. NRS 232.505 is hereby amended to read as follows:
12 232.505 As used in NRS 232.505 to 232.866, inclusive, and
13 sections 2 to 6, inclusive, of this act, unless the context requires
14 otherwise:
15 1. “Department” means the Department of Business and
16 Industry.
17 2. “Director” means the Director of the Department.
18 Sec. 8. NRS 232.8415 is hereby amended to read as follows:
19 232.8415 1. The Office [of Nevada Boards, Commissions
20 and Councils Standards] shall be responsible for:
21 (a) Centralized administration;
22 (b) A uniform set of standards for investigations, licensing
