EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to financial institutions; [eliminating an] revising provisions
relating to certain excise [tax] taxes on certain bank branches; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law imposes a $1,750 excise tax on a bank for each branch office maintained in
2 this State in excess of one branch office maintained by the bank in each county of this State on
3 the first day of each calendar quarter. Existing law requires the excise tax to be paid on or
4 before the last day of the first month of the calendar quarter. (NRS 363A.120) This bill
5 [provides for the repeal of these provisions on July 1, 2029.] : (1) imposes the $1,750 excise
6 tax on each branch office in excess of one branch office maintained by a bank in each
7 county of this State on July 1 each year; and (2) requires the excise tax to be paid on or
8 before July 31 each year.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. [NRS 363A.120 is hereby repealed.] (Deleted by amendment.)
2 Sec. 1.5. NRS 363A.120 is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 363A.120 1. There is hereby imposed an excise tax on each bank at the rate
4 of $1,750 for each branch office maintained by the bank in this State in excess of
5 one branch office maintained by the bank in each county in this State on [the first
6 day of each calendar quarter.] July 1 of each year.
7 2. Each bank that maintains more than one branch office in any county in this
8 State on [the first day of a calendar quarter] July 1, shall, on or before [the last day
9 of the first month of that calendar quarter:] July 31:
10 (a) File with the Department a return on a form prescribed by the Department;
11 and
12 (b) Remit to the Department any tax due pursuant to this section for the branch
13 offices maintained by the bank in this State on [the first day of that calendar
14 quarter.] July 1.

1 3. For the purposes of this section:
2 (a) “Bank” means:
3 (1) A corporation or limited-liability company that is chartered by this
4 State, another state or the United States which conducts banking or banking and
5 trust business; or
6 (2) A foreign bank licensed pursuant to chapter 666A of NRS.
7  The term does not include a financial institution engaging in business pursuant to
8 chapter 677 of NRS, a credit union organized under the provisions of chapter 672
9 of NRS or the Federal Credit Union Act, or any person or other entity this State is
10 prohibited from taxing under the Constitution, laws or treaties of the United States
11 or the Nevada Constitution.
12 (b) “Branch office” means any location or facility of a bank where deposit
13 accounts are opened, deposits are accepted, checks are paid and loans are granted,
14 including, but not limited to, a brick and mortar location, a detached or attached
15 drive-in facility, a seasonal office, an office on a military base or government
16 installation, a station or unit for paying and receiving, and a location where a
17 customer can open accounts, make deposits and borrow money by telephone or
18 through use of the Internet, and excluding any automated teller machines, consumer
19 credit offices, contractual offices, customer bank communication terminals,
20 electronic fund transfer units and loan production offices.
21 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, [2029.] 2027.
