Section 1:
S.B. 1
PREFILED SEPTEMBER 26, 2024
____________
Declared an Emergency Measure
SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to the Legislative Fund for
the costs of the 83rd Legislative Session.
(BDR S-523)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to the Legislative Fund for the
costs of the 83rd Legislative Session; and providing other
matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Legislative Fund created by NRS 218A.150 the sum of
3 $28,000,000 for the costs of the 83rd Legislative Session.
4 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
H
