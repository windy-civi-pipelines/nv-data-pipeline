SENATE BILL NO. 496–COMMITTEE ON FINANCE
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 15, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Department of Motor
Vehicles for information technology system projects.
(BDR S-1219)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Department of Motor
Vehicles for information technology system projects; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 Highway Fund to the Department of Motor Vehicles for the costs of
3 migrating a legacy information system to a cloud platform the
4 following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ............................. $13,780,212
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ............................... $9,792,437
7 2. The sums appropriated by subsection 1 are available for
8 either fiscal year and may be transferred from one fiscal year to the
9 other with the approval of the Interim Finance Committee upon the
10 recommendation of the Governor. Any remaining balance of those
11 sums must not be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by
12 the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to which
13 money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in
14 any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining
15 must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
-
*SB496_R2*
– 2 –
1 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
2 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and
3 must be reverted to the State Highway Fund on or before
4 September 17, 2027.
5 Sec. 2. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
6 Highway Fund to the Department of Motor Vehicles for the costs of
7 the continuation of the modernization project for the information
8 system of the Department the following sums:
9 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ............................. $33,903,370
10 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ............................. $39,687,596
11 2. The sums appropriated by subsection 1 are available for
12 either fiscal year and may be transferred from one fiscal year to the
13 other with the approval of the Interim Finance Committee upon the
14 recommendation of the Governor. Any remaining balance of those
15 sums must not be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by
16 the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to which
17 money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in
18 any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining
19 must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
20 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
21 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and
22 must be reverted to the State Highway Fund on or before
23 September 17, 2027.
24 Sec. 3. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
