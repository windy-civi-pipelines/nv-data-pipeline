_______________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Department of Motor Vehicles for
information technology system projects. (BDR S-1219)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Department of Motor Vehicles for
information technology system projects; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State Highway Fund to
2 the Department of Motor Vehicles for the costs of migrating a legacy information
3 system to a cloud platform the following sums:
4 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................................. $13,780,212
5 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ..................................................... $9,792,437
6 2. The sums appropriated by subsection 1 are available for either fiscal year
7 [.] and may be transferred from one fiscal year to the other with the approval
8 of the Interim Finance Committee upon the recommendation of the Governor.
9 Any remaining balance of those sums must not be committed for expenditure after
10 June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to
11 which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any
12 manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be spent for
13 any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was
14 appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently granted or
15 transferred, and must be reverted to the State Highway Fund on or before
16 September 17, 2027.
17 Sec. 2. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State Highway Fund to the
18 Department of Motor Vehicles for the costs of the continuation of the
19 modernization project for the information system of the Department the following
20 sums:
21 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ................................................... $33,903,370
