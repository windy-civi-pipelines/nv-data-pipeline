EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, and the
University of Nevada, Reno, for planning, design and upgrades [and
construction] for certain buildings; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, the sum of $6,200,000 for:
3 (a) The planning and design of a new building for the Lee Business School;
4 and
5 (b) Security, health and public safety upgrades to the facilities of the
6 University of Nevada, Las Vegas, campus.
7 2. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by subsection 1 must not
8 be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
9 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the appropriation is
10 granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
11 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
12 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the
13 money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State
14 General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
15 Sec. 2. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
16 University of Nevada, Reno, the sum of $6,200,000 for the [construction] planning
17 and design of a life sciences building.
18 2. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by subsection 1 must not
19 be committed for expenditure after June 30, [2029,] 2027, by the entity to which the
20 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the appropriation is
21 granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated

1 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September [21, 2029,] 17,
2 2027, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity to
3 which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to
4 the State General Fund on or before September [21, 2029.] 17, 2027.
5 Sec. 2.5. The State Controller shall transfer the sum of $12,400,000 from
6 the Consolidated Bond Interest and Redemption Fund created by NRS 349.090
7 to the State General Fund.
8 Sec. 3. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
