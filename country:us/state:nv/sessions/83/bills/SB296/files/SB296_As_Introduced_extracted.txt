Section 1:
S.B. 296
MARCH 6, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to governmental
administration. (BDR 19-944)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to governmental administration; prohibiting
governmental entities from entering into certain
nondisclosure or confidentiality agreements; declaring
certain records relating to a predecisional or deliberative
process of the governmental entity concerning a rule,
ordinance or regulation are public records; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law provides that, unless otherwise declared by law to be confidential,
2 all public books and records of a state or local governmental entity are required to
3 be open at all times during office hours for the public to inspect, copy or receive a
4 copy thereof. (NRS 239.010) Section 1 of this bill prohibits a governmental entity
5 from entering into a nondisclosure or confidentiality agreement unless the
6 information required to be kept confidential is otherwise made confidential
7 pursuant to state or federal law.
8 Section 2 of this bill declares that a record that pertains to a predecisional or
9 deliberative process between a governmental entity and a private person or entity
10 concerning a rule, ordinance or regulation which the governmental entity is
11 considering adopting is a public record.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 237 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. A governmental entity shall not enter into a nondisclosure
4 or confidentiality agreement unless the information that will be
-

1 kept confidential by the agreement is otherwise made confidential
2 pursuant to state or federal law.
3 2. As used in this section, “governmental entity” has the
4 meaning ascribed to it in NRS 239.005.
5 Sec. 2. Chapter 239 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
6 thereto a new section to read as follows:
7 1. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a record that
8 pertains to a predecisional or deliberative process between a
9 governmental entity and a private person or entity who is not
10 employed by or actively under contract with the governmental
11 entity concerning a rule, ordinance or regulation which the
12 governmental entity is considering adopting is a public record.
13 2. Nothing in this section shall be construed to deem a
14 predecisional or deliberative record of a governmental entity a
15 public record when the record was shared solely between
16 governmental entities or within a governmental entity.
17 Sec. 3. The amendatory provisions of this act do not apply to
18 any contract entered into before October 1, 2025.
