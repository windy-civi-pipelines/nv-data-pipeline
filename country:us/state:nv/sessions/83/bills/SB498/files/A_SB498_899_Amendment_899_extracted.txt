SENATE BILL NO. 498–COMMITTEE ON FINANCE
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 19, 2025
_______________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes [an appropriation] appropriations to the Nevada System of
Higher Education to expand undergraduate and graduate nursing
programs and for academic programming and building
renovations at institutions within the System. (BDR S-1200)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets omitted material is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making [an appropriation] appropriations to the Nevada System of
Higher Education to expand undergraduate and graduate nursing
programs and for academic programming and building renovations
at institutions within the System; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 the Nevada System of Higher Education to expand undergraduate and graduate
3 nursing programs at institutions within the Nevada System of Higher Education the
4 following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ................................................... $10,000,000
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ................................................... $10,000,000
7 2. The money appropriated by subsection 1:
8 (a) May be used only for operating costs associated with expanding such
9 undergraduate and graduate nursing programs, including, without limitation, costs
10 related to faculty, staff and equipment; and
11 (b) Must not be used for capital expenditures, including, without limitation, the
12 construction of new facilities.
13 3. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1 remaining at the end
14 of the respective fiscal years must not be committed for expenditure after June 30
15 of the respective fiscal years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any
16 entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in
Assembly Amendment No. 899 to Senate Bill No. 498 Page 4
1 any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be
2 spent for any purpose after September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027,
3 respectively, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
4 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted
5 to the State General Fund on or before September 18, 2026, and September 17,
6 2027, respectively.
7 Sec. 1.3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
8 the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of $2,500,000 for the Nevada
9 State University budget account to support academic programming at the
10 North Las Vegas site of the Nevada State University.
11 Sec. 1.5. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
12 the Nevada System of Higher Education the sum of $1,511,999 for the Western
13 Nevada College budget account for the renovation of the Aspen Building on
14 the campus of Western Nevada College.
15 Sec. 1.7. Any remaining balance of an appropriation made by section 1.3
16 or 1.5 of this act must not be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027,
17 by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to which money
18 from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner,
19 and any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be spent for
20 any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money
21 was appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently granted
22 or transferred, and must be reverted to the State General Fund on or before
23 September 17, 2027.
24 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
