Section 1:
EXEMPT
S.B. 452
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
APRIL 1, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Extends the date of reversion of certain previous
appropriations made to the Office of Finance in the
Office of the Governor. (BDR S-1152)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to state financial administration; extending the
date of reversion of the appropriations made by the 82nd
Session of the Nevada Legislature to the Office of
Finance in the Office of the Governor for the costs of
leases and furniture for state offices and training for state
employees; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 During the 2023 Legislative Session, the Nevada Legislature appropriated from
2 the State General Fund to the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor
3 $50,000,000 for the costs of leases and furniture for state offices and $5,000,000 for
4 training for state employees. Existing law requires that the appropriated money
5 revert to the State General Fund on or before September 19, 2025. (Chapter 341,
6 Statutes of Nevada 2023, at page 1954) This bill extends the reversion date for
7 these appropriations to on or before September 17, 2027.
-
*SB452*
– 2 –
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Section 3 of chapter 341, Statutes of Nevada 2023,
2 at page 1954, is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 Sec. 3. Any remaining balance of the appropriations
4 made by sections 1 and 2 of this act must not be committed
5 for expenditure after June 30, [2025,] 2027, by the entity to
6 which the appropriation is made or any entity to which money
7 from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in
8 any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money
9 remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September
10 [19, 2025,] 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money
11 was appropriated or the entity to which the money was
12 subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to
13 the State General Fund on or before September [19, 2025.]
14 17, 2027.
15 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
H
