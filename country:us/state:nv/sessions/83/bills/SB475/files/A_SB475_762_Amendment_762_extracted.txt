(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 15, 2025
_______________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Division of State Parks of the State
Department of Conservation and Natural Resources for the
purchase and replacement of vehicles, equipment, computer and
information technology equipment and radios and for certain
projects. (BDR S-1216)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Division of State Parks of the State
Department of Conservation and Natural Resources for the purchase
and replacement of vehicles, equipment, computer and information
technology equipment and radios and for certain projects; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 the Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
3 Resources for the replacement of computers and information technology equipment
4 and the repair and replacement of radios the following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ........................................................ $113,586
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ........................................................ $122,560
7 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1 remaining at the
8 end of the respective fiscal years must not be committed for expenditure after June
9 30 of the respective fiscal years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or
10 any entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
11 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining
12 must not be spent for any purpose after September 18, 2026, and September 17,
13 2027, respectively, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the
14 entity to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be
Senate Amendment No. 762 to Senate Bill No. 475 Page 4
1 reverted to the State General Fund on or before September 18, 2026, and September
2 17, 2027, respectively.
3 Sec. 2. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
4 Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
5 Resources for improvement and deferred maintenance projects the following sums:
6 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ..................................................... $2,500,000
7 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ..................................................... $2,500,000
8 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1 remaining at the end
9 of the respective fiscal years must not be committed for expenditure after June 30
10 of the respective fiscal years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any
11 entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in
12 any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be
13 spent for any purpose after September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027,
14 respectively, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
15 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted
16 to the State General Fund on or before September 18, 2026, and September 17,
17 2027, respectively.
18 Sec. 3. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
19 Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
20 Resources for the purchase of computer hardware and associated software,
21 furniture, vehicles and law enforcement equipment for new ranger positions the
22 following sums:
23 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 ..................................... [$882,713] $517,965
24 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 ......................................... [$17,236] $31,987
25 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1 remaining at the
26 end of the respective fiscal years must not be committed for expenditure after June
27 30 of the respective fiscal years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or
28 any entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
29 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated money remaining
30 must not be spent for any purpose after September 18, 2026, and September 17,
31 2027, respectively, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the
32 entity to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be
33 reverted to the State General Fund on or before September 18, 2026, and September
34 17, 2027, respectively.
35 Sec. 4. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
36 Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
37 Resources the sum of $5,550,554 for the replacement of vehicles.
38 Sec. 5. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
39 Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
40 Resources the sum of $1,367,377 for the replacement of utility vehicles, excavators
41 and tractors.
42 Sec. 6. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
43 Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
44 Resources the sum of $24,780 for the replacement of diagnostic automotive
45 equipment.
46 Sec. 7. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
47 Division of State Parks of the State Department of Conservation and Natural
48 Resources the sum of $91,229 for the replacement of mowers and small field
49 equipment.
50 Sec. 8. Any remaining balance of the appropriations made by sections 4 to 7,
51 inclusive, of this act must not be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by
52 the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the
53 appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of
Senate Amendment No. 762 to Senate Bill No. 475 Page 5
1 the appropriated money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
2 September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or
3 the entity to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be
4 reverted to the State General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
5 Sec. 9. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
