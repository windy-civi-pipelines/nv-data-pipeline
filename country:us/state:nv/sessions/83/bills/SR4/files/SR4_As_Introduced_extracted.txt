Section 1:
S.R. 4
MARCH 6, 2025
____________
Read and Adopted
SUMMARY—Provides for the compensation of the clergy in the
Senate for the 83rd Session of the Nevada
Legislature. (BDR R-1151)
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
SENATE RESOLUTION—Providing for the compensation of the
clergy and the coordinator of the clergy for services
rendered to the Senate during the 83rd Session of the
Nevada Legislature.
1 WHEREAS, The members of the 83rd Session of the Nevada
2 Legislature sincerely appreciate the daily religious services that
3 have been rendered by members of the clergy representing various
4 denominations; and
5 WHEREAS, The invocations offered by the clergy provide
6 inspiration and guidance for the members of the Senate as they face
7 the challenges and demands of a legislative session; and
8 WHEREAS, The assistance provided by the coordinator of the
9 clergy has facilitated the daily services; and
10 WHEREAS, A reasonable compensation should be provided for
11 the clergy who perform such services and for the coordinator of the
12 clergy; now, therefore, be it
13 RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, That the
14 State Controller is authorized and directed to pay the sum of $40 per
15 service out of the Legislative Fund to the members of the clergy
16 who performed religious services for the Senate during the 83rd
17 Session of the Nevada Legislature; and be it further
18 RESOLVED, That the State Controller is authorized and directed
19 to pay the sum of $1,500 to the coordinator of the clergy who
20 facilitated the services for the Senate during the 83rd Session of the
21 Nevada Legislature; and be it further
-
*SR4*
– 2 –
1 RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
2 adoption.
