ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 554–COMMITTEE ON WAYS AND MEANS
MAY 8, 2025
_______________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to the Grant Matching Account.
(BDR 18-1227)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to financial administration; providing that any money remaining
in the Grant Matching Account at the end of the fiscal year does not
revert to the State General Fund; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law creates the Nevada Grant Matching Program within the Office of Federal
2 Assistance in the Office of the Governor to provide funds as grants to state agencies, local
3 agencies, tribal governments and nonprofit organizations for the purpose of satisfying the
4 matching funds requirement for a federal grant. (NRS 223.490) Existing law also: (1) creates
5 the Grant Matching Account as part of the Program; and (2) requires money in the Account,
6 except for money received from a grant, gift or donation to the Account, that has not been
7 committed for expenditure on or before June 30 of an odd-numbered fiscal year to revert to
8 the State General Fund. (NRS 223.492) This bill provides that any money remaining in the
9 Account at the end of a fiscal year does not revert to the State General Fund and requires the
10 balance in the Account to be carried forward to the next fiscal year.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 223.492 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 223.492 1. The Grant Matching Account is hereby created in the State
3 General Fund. The Office shall administer the Grant Matching Account.
4 2. Money received from:
5 (a) A direct legislative appropriation to the Grant Matching Account;
6 (b) A transfer from the Abandoned Property Trust Account pursuant to NRS
7 120A.620; and
8 (c) A grant, gift or donation to the Grant Matching Account,
9  must be deposited in the Grant Matching Account. The interest and income
10 earned on the money in the Grant Matching Account must be credited to the Grant
11 Matching Account.
Assembly Amendment No. 818 to Assembly Bill No. 554 Page 4
1 3. [Except as otherwise provided in subsection 4, the balance] Any money
2 remaining in the Grant Matching Account [that has not been committed for
3 expenditure on or before June 30 of an odd-numbered fiscal year reverts] at the end
4 of a fiscal year does not revert to the State General Fund [.] , and the balance in
5 the Account must be carried forward to the next fiscal year.
6 4. All money received from a grant, gift or donation to the Grant Matching
7 Account:
8 (a) Must be accounted for separately in the Account; and
9 (b) Must be expended in accordance with the terms of the gift, grant or
10 donation . [; and
11 (c) Does not revert to the State General Fund and must be carried over into the
12 next fiscal year.]
13 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective [on July 1, 2025.] upon passage and
14 approval.
