SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to the compensation of
state employees. (BDR S-1229)

EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.

allowed salaries for certain state employees; revising
provisions relating to the compensation of employees of
the Senate and Assembly; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
