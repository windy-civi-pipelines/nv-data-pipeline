EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to emergency response; creating the Account for the Nevada
School Safety Data Grant Program within the [Department of Public
Safety] Division of Emergency Management of the Office of the
Military to award grants to [school districts] law enforcement
agencies to procure school mapping data for public schools in this
State; prescribing certain requirements for the use of grants received
from the Account; [making an appropriation;] authorizing the
installation of master key boxes at public and private schools; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires: (1) the board of trustees of each school district and the governing
2 body of each charter school and private school to establish a development committee to
3 develop a plan to be used by the schools in the district or the charter school or private school
4 in responding to a crisis, emergency or suicide; and (2) such a development committee to
5 annually review and, as appropriate, update the plan. (NRS 388.241, 388.245, 394.1685,
6 394.1688) Existing law creates the Division of Emergency Management within the Office
7 of the Military and requires the Chief of the Division to carry out the program for
8 emergency management in this State. (NRS 414.040) Section 1 of this bill creates the
9 Account for the Nevada School Safety Data Grant Program within the [Department of Public
10 Safety] Division to award grants to [school districts] law enforcement agencies in this State
11 to procure school mapping data for [each] public [school and private school] schools located
12 in the county in which the [school district] jurisdiction of the law enforcement agency is
13 located. Section 1: (1) authorizes a [school district] law enforcement agency to apply for
14 grants from the Program; (2) requires any grant money awarded from the Program to be used
15 for purposes of providing school mapping data [, including, without limitation,] by
16 contracting with a vendor to provide school mapping data; (3) requires an entity that produces
17 school mapping data to meet certain requirements and to provide the school mapping data
18 to the school district and any public safety agency in the county in which the school district is
19 located; (4) requires such school mapping data to comply with certain requirements; and (5)
Assembly Amendment No. 293 to Assembly Bill No. 412 Page 4
20 requires the [Department] Division to adopt regulations to carry out the provisions of the
21 Program. [Section 2 of this bill makes an appropriation of $2,500,000 to the Account for the
22 purposes of making grants from the Account.] Sections 1 and 1.1 of this bill prohibit the
23 disclosure of school mapping data produced using a grant from the Account except to
24 employees of the school district, a public safety agency or the Division.
25 Sections 1.3 and 1.7 of this bill authorize the board of trustees of a school district or
26 the governing body of a charter school or private school to provide for the installation at
27 the schools under the management of the board of trustees or the governing body of a
28 secure, exterior master key box meeting certain requirements. Sections 1.3 and 1.7
29 require the board of trustees or the governing body to consult with public safety agencies
30 to determine the location to install the master key box. Sections 1.5 and 1.9 of this bill
31 make certain existing definitions relating to emergency responses in schools applicable to
32 sections 1.3 and 1.7.

32 such school mapping data.

School Safety Data Grant Program within the [Department of Public
Safety] Division of Emergency Management of the Office of the
