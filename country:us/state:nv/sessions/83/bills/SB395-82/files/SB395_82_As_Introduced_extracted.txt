Section 1:
REQUIRES TWO-THIRDS MAJORITY VOTE (§ 1) EXEMPT
THIRD REPRINT S.B. 395
SENATE BILL NO. 395–SENATOR NEAL
MARCH 27, 2023
____________
Referred to Committee on Judiciary
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to real property.
(BDR 10-288)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: May have Fiscal Impact.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to real property; limiting, with certain exceptions,
the total aggregate number of units of residential real
property in this State that may be purchased in any 1
calendar year by certain corporate investors; requiring the
registration of certain corporate investors in residential
property in this State with the Securities Division of the
Office of the Secretary of State; requiring that certain
deeds relating to residential real property include certain
information about corporate investors; making an
appropriation; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Section 1 of this bill provides, with certain exceptions, that: (1) the total
2 aggregate number of units of residential real property in this State that may be
3 purchased in any 1 calendar year by corporations, limited-liability companies and
4 any affiliates of such entities must not exceed 1,000 units; and (2) a corporation,
5 limited-liability company or affiliate of such an entity is prohibited from purchasing
6 any unit of residential real property in this State if, as a result of the purchase, the
7 total aggregate number of units of residential real property purchased in this State
8 during the current calendar year by corporations, limited-liability companies or any
9 affiliates of such entities would exceed 1,000 units.
10 Section 1 also requires the creation and maintenance of a registry of
11 corporations, limited-liability companies and any affiliates of such entities that
12 purchase or own residential real property in this State by the Securities Division of
13 the Office of the Secretary of State. Section 1 requires, with certain exceptions, a
14 corporation, limited-liability company or any affiliate of such an entity to register
15 with the Securities Division before purchasing any residential real property in this
16 State. Section 1: (1) authorizes the Secretary of State to charge a fee to each such
17 corporation, limited-liability company or any affiliate of such an entity; and (2)
-
*SB395_R3*
– 2 –
18 requires the Secretary of State to adopt regulations necessary to carry out the
19 provisions of section 1. For the purposes of section 1, the term “corporation” does
20 not include a family trust company or a housing authority.
21 Section 2 of this bill provides, with certain exceptions, that if a corporation,
22 limited-liability company or any affiliate of such an entity purchases residential real
23 property, the deed must: (1) be accompanied by a copy of the certificate of
24 registration issued by the Secretary of State; and (2) clearly set forth that the
25 residential real property is not the primary residence of the owner. Section 2 also
26 prohibits the county recorder from recording the deed unless the deed: (1) contains
27 information about the ownership of the corporation, limited-liability company or
28 any affiliate of such an entity, as set forth in the registry created pursuant to section
29 1; and (2) clearly sets forth that the residential real property is not the primary
30 residence of the owner.
31 Section 3 of this bill makes an appropriation to the Office of the Secretary of
32 State for personnel, travel, operating, equipment, information services and
33 contractor costs to carry out the provisions of section 1.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 111 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. Except as otherwise provided in subsection 2:
4 (a) The total aggregate number of units of residential real
5 property in this State that may be purchased in any 1 calendar
6 year by corporations, limited-liability companies and affiliates of
7 such entities must not exceed 1,000 units.
8 (b) A corporation, limited-liability company or affiliate of such
9 an entity shall not purchase any unit of residential real property in
10 this State if, as a result of the purchase, the total aggregate
11 number of units of residential real property purchased in this State
12 during the current calendar year by corporations, limited-liability
13 companies or affiliates of such entities would exceed 1,000 units.
14 2. The provisions of subsection 1 do not apply to:
15 (a) The intracorporate sale or transfer of units of residential
16 property; or
17 (b) The sale of newly constructed units of residential property.
18 3. The Securities Division of the Office of the Secretary of
19 State shall create and maintain a registry of corporations, limited-
20 liability companies and affiliates of such entities that purchase or
21 own residential real property in this State. The Securities Division
22 shall make such registry available on the Internet website of the
23 Office of the Secretary of State.
24 4. Except as otherwise provided in this subsection, a
25 corporation, limited-liability company or affiliate of such an entity
26 must register with the Securities Division of the Office of the
27 Secretary of State before purchasing any unit of residential real

2 to a corporation, limited-liability company or affiliate of such an
3 entity that owns less than 10 units of residential real property in
4 this State.
5 5. The Secretary of State shall issue a certificate of
6 registration to each corporation, limited liability company or
7 affiliate of such an entity that registers pursuant to this section.
8 6. The Secretary of State may charge a fee to each
9 corporation, limited-liability company or affiliate of such an entity
10 that registers with the Securities Division pursuant to subsection 4.
11 7. The Secretary of State shall adopt any regulations
12 necessary to carry out the provisions of this section.
13 8. As used in this section, the term:
14 (a) “Corporation” does not include:
15 (1) A family trust company, as defined in NRS 669.042.
16 (2) A housing authority, as defined in NRS 315.021.
17 (b) “Limited-liability company” has the meaning ascribed to it
18 in NRS 86.061.
19 Sec. 2. NRS 111.312 is hereby amended to read as follows:
20 111.312 1. The county recorder shall not record with respect
21 to real property, a notice of completion, a declaration of homestead,
22 a declaration of removal of discriminatory restriction, a lien or

24 conveyance of real property or instrument in writing setting forth an

32 of the document, if the county assessor has assigned a parcel
33 number to the property. The parcel number must comply with the
34 current system for numbering parcels used by the county assessor’s
35 office. The county recorder is not required to verify that the
36 assessor’s parcel number is correct.
37 2. Any document relating exclusively to the transfer of water
38 rights may be recorded without containing the assessor’s parcel
39 number of the property.

5 complete legal description of the real property conveyed.
6 5. Except as otherwise provided in subsection 6, if a document
7 that is being recorded includes a legal description of real property
8 that is provided in metes and bounds, the document must include the
9 name and mailing address of the person who prepared the legal
10 description. The county recorder is not required to verify the

17 recorded. The county recorder is not required to verify the accuracy

21 purchases residential real property:

23 (1) The deed contains the information about the ownership

25 entity set forth in the registry created pursuant to section 1 of this
26 act; and
27 (2) The corporation, limited-liability company or affiliate of
28 such an entity submits to the county recorder a copy of the
29 certificate of registration issued by the Secretary of State pursuant

32 the name on the certificate of registration accompanying the deed;
33 and
34 (b) The deed must clearly set forth that the residential real
35 property is not the primary residence of the owner.
36 8. The provisions of subsection 7 do not apply to a
37 corporation, limited-liability company or affiliate of such an entity
38 that owns less than 10 units of residential real property in this
39 State.
40 9. As used in this section, the terms “corporation” and
41 “limited-liability company” have the meanings ascribed to them in
42 section 1 of this act.
