Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Office of the Secretary of State for
costs associated with the voter registration and elections
management system and connectivity of automatic voter
registration agencies. (BDR S-1180)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Office of the Secretary of State for costs
associated with the voter registration and elections management system
and connectivity of automatic voter registration agencies; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
2 Office of the Secretary of State the sum of [$22,504,162] $27,004,162 for the
3 HAVA Election Reform budget account for the costs of the continued replacement
4 of the voter registration and elections management system.
5 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
6 Office of the Secretary of State the sum of $748,800 for the HAVA Election
7 Reform budget account for the costs of consulting services to support the continued
8 implementation of the voter registration and elections management system.
9 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to the
10 Office of the Secretary of State the sum of $3,487,364 for the HAVA Election
11 Reform budget account for the costs of connectivity of automatic voter registration
12 agencies.
13 Sec. 4. Any remaining balance of an appropriation made by sections 1, 2 and
14 3 of this act must not be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the
15 entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the
16 appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of
17 the appropriated money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
Assembly Amendment No. 813 to Assembly Bill No. 562 Page 4
1 September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money was appropriated or
2 the entity to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be
3 reverted to the fund from which it was appropriated on or before September 17,
4 2027.
5 Sec. 5. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
