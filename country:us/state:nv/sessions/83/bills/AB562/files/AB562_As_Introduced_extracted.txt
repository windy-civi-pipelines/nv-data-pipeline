Section 1:
EXEMPT
A.B. 562
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 14, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Office of the Secretary
of State for costs associated with the voter
registration and elections management system and
connectivity of automatic voter registration agencies.
(BDR S-1180)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Office of the Secretary of
State for costs associated with the voter registration and
elections management system and connectivity of
automatic voter registration agencies; and providing other
matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Office of the Secretary of State the sum of $22,504,162
3 for the HAVA Election Reform budget account for the costs of the
4 continued replacement of the voter registration and elections
5 management system.
6 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
7 Fund to the Office of the Secretary of State the sum of $748,800 for
8 the HAVA Election Reform budget account for the costs of
9 consulting services to support the continued implementation of the
10 voter registration and elections management system.
-
*AB562*
– 2 –
1 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Office of the Secretary of State the sum of $3,487,364
3 for the HAVA Election Reform budget account for the costs of
4 connectivity of automatic voter registration agencies.
5 Sec. 4. Any remaining balance of an appropriation made by
6 sections 1, 2 and 3 of this act must not be committed for expenditure
7 after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made
8 or any entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or
9 otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the
10 appropriated money remaining must not be spent for any purpose
11 after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money
12 was appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently
13 granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the fund from which
14 it was appropriated on or before September 17, 2027.
15 Sec. 5. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
H
