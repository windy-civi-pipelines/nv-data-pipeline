Section 1:
A.B. 173
PREFILED FEBRUARY 2, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to judicial office.
(BDR 23-102)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to elections; requiring a candidate for judicial
office to indicate his or her designated political party
affiliation on certain financial disclosure statements; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 The Nevada Financial Disclosure Act requires a candidate for nomination,
2 election or reelection to a public office for which the candidate will be entitled to
3 receive annual compensation of $6,000 or more for serving in the office to file a
4 financial disclosure statement that contains certain information not later than the
5 10th day after the last day to qualify as a candidate for the office. (NRS 281.561,
6 281.571) Section 1 of this bill requires a candidate for judicial office to include an
7 indication of his or her designated political party affiliation on such a financial
8 disclosure statement. Section 2 of this bill places section 1 within the Nevada
9 Financial Disclosure Act.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 281 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. In addition to the requirements of NRS 281.571, a
4 candidate for judicial office must indicate on the financial
5 disclosure statement filed pursuant to paragraph (a) of subsection
6 1 of NRS 281.561 the designation of his or her political party
7 affiliation for the purposes of voter registration.
8 2. As used in this section, “judicial office” has the meaning
9 ascribed to it in NRS 293.064.
-
*AB173*
– 2 –
1 Sec. 2. NRS 281.5555 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 281.5555 The provisions of NRS 281.5555 to 281.581,
3 inclusive, and section 1 of this act, may be cited as the Nevada
4 Financial Disclosure Act.
5 Sec. 3. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
