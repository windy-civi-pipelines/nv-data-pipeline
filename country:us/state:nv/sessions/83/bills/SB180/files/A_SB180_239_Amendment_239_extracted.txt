SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to motor carriers. (BDR 58-963)

EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to motor carriers; establishing the minimum amount of insurance
against liability of which the Nevada Transportation Authority of the
Department of Business and Industry and the Department of Motor
Vehicles must require certain motor carriers to file evidence, bond or
surety; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law authorizes the Nevada Transportation Authority of the Department of
2 Business and Industry and the Department of Motor Vehicles to require certain motor carriers
3 to file with the Authority or the Department, as applicable, in a form required and approved by
4 the Authority or the Department a liability insurance policy, or a certificate of insurance in
5 lieu thereof, or a bond of a surety company, or other surety, in such reasonable sum as the
6 Authority or the Department may deem necessary to protect adequately the interests of the
7 public. (NRS 706.291) This bill requires the Authority or the Department to require the filing
8 of such a policy, certificate, bond or other surety, in an amount not less than [$1,500,000] a
9 certain minimum amount, by: (1) every fully regulated carrier which is subject to regulation
10 by the Authority and authorized to transport only freight; and (2) every other common and
11 contract motor carrier and every private carrier which is subject to regulation by the
12 Department and authorized to transport intrastate-only freight by vehicles with a
13 manufacturer’s gross vehicle weight rating of 26,000 pounds or more. Under this bill, the
14 minimum amount of such a policy, certificate, bond or other surety is required to be: (1)
15 on or after January 1, 2026, $1,000,000; (2) on or after January 1, 2028, $1,250,000; and
16 (3) on or after January 1, 2030, $1,500,000.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 706.291 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 706.291 1. [The] Except as otherwise provided in this subsection, the
3 Authority shall require every fully regulated carrier and every operator of a tow car,
4 within such time and in such amounts as the Authority may designate, to file with
5 the Authority in a form required and approved by the Authority a liability insurance
6 policy, or a certificate of insurance in lieu thereof, or a bond of a surety company,

1 or other surety, in such reasonable sum as the Authority may deem necessary to
2 protect adequately the interests of the public. The Authority shall require every
3 fully regulated carrier which is authorized to transport only freight to file with
4 the Authority in a form required and approved by the Authority a liability
5 insurance policy, or a certificate of insurance in lieu thereof, or a bond of a
6 surety company, or other surety [,] :
7 (a) On or after January 1, 2026, in an amount not less than $1,000,000;
8 (b) On or after January 1, 2028, in an amount not less than $1,250,000; and
9 (c) On or after January 1, 2030, in an amount not less than $1,500,000.
10 2. [The] Except as otherwise provided in this subsection, the Department
11 shall require every other common and contract motor carrier and every private
12 carrier, within such time and in such amounts as the Department may designate, to
13 file with the Department in a form required and approved by the Department a
14 liability insurance policy, or a certificate of insurance in lieu thereof, a bond of a
15 surety company, or other surety, in such reasonable sum as the Department may
16 deem necessary to protect adequately the interests of the public. In determining the
17 amount of liability insurance or other surety required of a carrier pursuant to this
18 subsection, the Department shall [create] :
19 (a) Create a separate category for vehicles with a manufacturer’s gross vehicle
20 weight rating of less than 26,000 pounds and impose a lesser requirement with
21 respect to such vehicles [.] ; and
22 (b) Require every other common and contract motor carrier and every
23 private carrier which is authorized to transport intrastate-only freight by the
24 category of vehicles with a manufacturer’s gross vehicle weight rating of 26,000
25 pounds or more to file with the Department in a form required and approved by
26 the Department a liability insurance policy, or a certificate of insurance in lieu
27 thereof, or a bond of a surety company, or other surety [,] :
28 (1) On or after January 1, 2026, in an amount not less than $1,000,000;
29 (2) On or after January 1, 2028, in an amount not less than $1,250,000;
30 and
31 (3) On or after January 1, 2030, in an amount not less than $1,500,000.
32 3. The liability insurance policy or certificate, policy or bond of a surety
33 company or other surety must bind the obligors thereunder to pay the compensation
34 for injuries to persons or for loss or damage to property resulting from the negligent
35 operation of the carrier.
36 4. The Authority and the Department may jointly prescribe by regulation the
37 respective amounts and forms required by subsections 1 and 2.
38 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective:
39 1. Upon passage and approval for the purpose of adopting any regulations and
40 performing any other preparatory administrative tasks that are necessary to carry
41 out the provisions of this act; and
42 2. On October 1, 2025, for all other purposes.
