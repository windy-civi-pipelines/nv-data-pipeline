Section 1:
EXEMPT
A.B. 577
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 14, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the State Department of
Conservation and Natural Resources for a certain
new website and database and for the replacement of
vehicles and computer hardware and associated
software. (BDR S-1215)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the State Department of
Conservation and Natural Resources for a certain new
website and database and for the replacement of vehicles
and computer hardware and associated software; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the State Department of Conservation and Natural
3 Resources the sum of $257,400 for the Conservation and Natural
4 Resources Administration budget account for a new website and
5 online database for the Sagebrush Ecosystem Technical Team.
6 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
7 Fund to the State Department of Conservation and Natural
8 Resources the sum of $144,032 for the Conservation Districts
9 Program budget account for the replacement of vehicles.
10 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
11 Fund to the Division of Water Resources of the State Department of
-
*AB577*
– 2 –
1 Conservation and Natural Resources the sum of $129,539 for the
2 replacement of vehicles.
3 Sec. 4. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
4 Fund to the Division of Water Resources of the State Department of
5 Conservation and Natural Resources the sum of $15,953 for the
6 replacement of computer hardware and associated software.
7 Sec. 5. Any remaining balance of the appropriations made by
8 sections 1 to 4, inclusive, of this act must not be committed for
9 expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
10 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the
11 appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and
12 any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be spent
13 for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to
14 which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the money
15 was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the
16 State General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
17 Sec. 6. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
