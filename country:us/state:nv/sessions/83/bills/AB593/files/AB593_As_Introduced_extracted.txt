Section 1:
EXEMPT
A.B. 593
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 21, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Interim Finance
Committee for costs associated with the
implementation of the Enterprise Resource Planning
System. (BDR S-1223)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Interim Finance Committee
for costs associated with the implementation of the
Enterprise Resource Planning System; and providing
other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Interim Finance Committee the sum of $53,434,995 for
3 allocation to the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor for
4 costs associated with the implementation of the Enterprise Resource
5 Planning System.
6 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State Highway
7 Fund to the Interim Finance Committee the sum of $12,534,135 for
8 allocation to the Office of Finance in the Office of the Governor for
9 costs associated with the implementation of the Enterprise Resource
10 Planning System.
11 Sec. 3. Any remaining balance of an appropriation made by
12 section 1 or 2 of this act must not be committed for expenditure after
13 June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the appropriation is made or
-
*AB593*
– 2 –
1 any entity to which money from the appropriation is granted or
2 otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the
3 appropriated money remaining must not be spent for any purpose
4 after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to which the money
5 was appropriated or the entity to which the money was subsequently
6 granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the fund from which
7 it was appropriated on or before September 17, 2027.
8 Sec. 4. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
