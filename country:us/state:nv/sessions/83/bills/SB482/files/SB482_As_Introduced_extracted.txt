Section 1:
EXEMPT
S.B. 482
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 15, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes a supplemental appropriation to the Nevada
Gaming Control Board for an unanticipated shortfall
related to personnel expenses. (BDR S-1225)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included
in Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making a supplemental appropriation to the Nevada
Gaming Control Board for an unanticipated shortfall
related to personnel expenses; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to the Nevada Gaming Control Board the sum of $1,060,682
3 for an unanticipated shortfall related to personnel expenses. This
4 appropriation is supplemental to that made by subsection 2 of
5 section 3 of chapter 148, Statutes of Nevada 2023, at page 748.
6 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
H
