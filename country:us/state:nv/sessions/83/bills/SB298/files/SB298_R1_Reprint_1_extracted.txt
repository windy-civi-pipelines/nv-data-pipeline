Section 1:
FIRST REPRINT S.B. 298
SENATE BILL NO. 298–COMMITTEE ON GOVERNMENT AFFAIRS
MARCH 6, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to peace officers.
(BDR 23-1031)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to peace officers; revising the definition of
“punitive action” as the term relates to certain peace
officers; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law: (1) prohibits a law enforcement agency from taking certain types
2 of punitive action against a peace officer; and (2) provides certain protections to
3 peace officers when punitive action may be imposed against them. (NRS 289.020,
4 289.057, 289.060, 289.080, 289.085, 289.092, 289.820, 289.824) Existing law
5 defines “punitive action” as any action which may lead to dismissal, demotion,
6 suspension, reduction in salary, written reprimand or transfer of a peace officer for
7 purposes of punishment. (NRS 289.010) Section 2 of this bill revises the definition
8 to include any action which may lead to denial of an increase, either in seniority or
9 compensation, for purposes of punishment.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. (Deleted by amendment.)
2 Sec. 2. NRS 289.010 is hereby amended to read as follows:
3 289.010 As used in this chapter, unless the context otherwise
4 requires:
5 1. “Administrative file” means any file of a peace officer
6 containing information, comments or documents about the peace
7 officer. The term does not include any file relating to an
8 investigation conducted pursuant to NRS 289.057 or a criminal
9 investigation of a peace officer.
-

– 2 –
1 2. “Adult use of cannabis” has the meaning ascribed to it in
2 NRS 678A.075.
3 3. “Law enforcement agency” means any agency, office,
4 bureau, department, unit or division created by any statute,
5 ordinance or rule which:
6 (a) Has a duty to enforce the law; and
7 (b) Employs any person upon whom some or all of the powers
8 of a peace officer are conferred pursuant to NRS 289.150 to
9 289.360, inclusive.
10 4. “Medical use of cannabis” has the meaning ascribed to it in
11 NRS 678A.215.
12 5. “Peace officer” means any person upon whom some or all of
13 the powers of a peace officer are conferred pursuant to NRS
14 289.150 to 289.360, inclusive.
15 6. “Punitive action” means any action which may lead to
16 dismissal, demotion, suspension, reduction in salary, written
17 reprimand , [or] transfer or denial of an increase, either in seniority
18 or compensation, of a peace officer for purposes of punishment.
19 7. “Screening test” means a test of a person’s blood, urine, hair
20 or saliva to detect the general presence of a controlled substance or
21 other drug.
