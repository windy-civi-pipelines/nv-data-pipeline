Section 1:
A.B. 132
PREFILED JANUARY 28, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Natural Resources
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to water. (BDR 48-586)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to water; revising the exemption from certain
appropriation requirements for guzzlers providing water
for use by wildlife; and providing other matters properly
relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law, under certain circumstances, exempts from the requirements of
2 the Nevada Revised Statutes governing the appropriation of water the de minimus
3 collection of precipitation in a guzzler to provide water for use by wildlife if the
4 guzzler has: (1) a capacity of 20,000 gallons or less; (2) a capture area of 1 acre or
5 less; and (3) a pipe length of 1/4 mile or less. (NRS 533.027) This bill increases
6 the: (1) maximum allowed capacity of the guzzler to 40,000 gallons; and (2)
7 maximum allowed pipe length of the guzzler to 1/2 mile.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. NRS 533.027 is hereby amended to read as follows:
2 533.027 1. The provisions of this chapter do not apply to:
3 (a) The use of water in emergency situations to extinguish fires
4 by a public agency or a volunteer fire department; or
5 (b) The de minimus collection of precipitation:
6 (1) From the rooftop of a single-family dwelling for
7 nonpotable domestic use; or
8 (2) If the collection does not conflict with any existing water
9 rights as determined by the State Engineer, in a guzzler to provide
10 water for use by wildlife. The guzzler must:
11 (I) Have a capacity of [20,000] 40,000 gallons or less;
-

– 2 –
1 (II) Have a capture area of 1 acre or less;
2 (III) Have a pipe length of [1/4] 1/2 mile or less;
3 (IV) Be developed by a state or federal agency
4 responsible for wildlife management or by any other person in
5 consultation with the Department of Wildlife; and
6 (V) Be approved for use by the Department of Wildlife.
7 2. As used in this section:
8 (a) “Domestic use” has the meaning ascribed to it in
9 NRS 534.013.
10 (b) “Guzzler” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 501.121.
11 (c) “Public agency” means an agency, bureau, board,
12 commission, department or division of this State or a political
13 subdivision of this State.
