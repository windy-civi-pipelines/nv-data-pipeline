S.B. 469
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 15, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Finance
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to the Department of
Corrections for various purposes. (BDR S-1205)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to the Department of Corrections
for maintenance of certain buildings, the replacement of
certain vehicles, furniture and equipment and for deferred
maintenance projects; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
2 General Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Northern
3 Nevada Correctional Center budget account for the replacement of
4 security and safety equipment the following sums:
5 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................... $36,982
6 For the Fiscal Year 2026-2027 .................................... $34,980
7 2. Any balance of the sums appropriated by subsection 1
8 remaining at the end of the respective fiscal years must not be
9 committed for expenditure after June 30 of the respective fiscal
10 years by the entity to which the appropriation is made or any entity
11 to which money from the appropriation is granted or otherwise
12 transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
13 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after
14 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively, by
-

1 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity
2 to which the money was subsequently granted or transferred, and

4 September 18, 2026, and September 17, 2027, respectively.
5 Sec. 2. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
6 General Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Northern
7 Nevada Correctional Center budget account for the replacement of
8 culinary, maintenance and building equipment the following sums:
9 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................. $108,047

24 General Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Southern

26 utility vehicles, handheld metal detectors and other small equipment
27 the following sums:
28 For the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 .................................. $236,583

31 remaining at the end of the respective fiscal years must not be
32 committed for expenditure after June 30 of the respective fiscal

2 Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Northern Nevada

4 replacement of utility vehicles and warehouse equipment.

6 Fund to the Department of Corrections for the Southern Desert

8 maintenance projects.

15 Correctional Center budget account the sum of $120,434 for the
16 replacement of warehouse, maintenance and security equipment.

18 sections 4 to 8, inclusive, of this act must not be committed for

20 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the
21 appropriation is granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and
22 any portion of the appropriated money remaining must not be spent
23 for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by either the entity to
24 which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the money
25 was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the
26 State General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
27 Sec. 10. This act becomes effective on July 1, 2025.
