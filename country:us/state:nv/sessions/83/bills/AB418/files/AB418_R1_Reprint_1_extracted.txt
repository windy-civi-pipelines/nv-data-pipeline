Section 1:
FIRST REPRINT A.B. 418
ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 418–ASSEMBLYMEMBERS
HIBBETTS, HARDY; AND NGUYEN
MARCH 13, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Government Affairs
SUMMARY—Revises provisions relating to animals.
(BDR 20-849)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: May have Fiscal Impact.
Effect on the State: No.
CONTAINS UNFUNDED MANDATE (§§ 1, 2)
(NOT REQUESTED BY AFFECTED LOCAL GOVERNMENT)
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to animals; requiring each governing body of a
county or incorporated city to adopt an ordinance
requiring, with certain exceptions, the owner or operator
of a kennel, an animal rescue organization that handles
pets or a commercial establishment engaged in the
business of handling pets to complete a training course on
handling pets prescribed by the governing body and
renew the training biennially; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law grants a governing body of a county or incorporated city all
2 powers necessary and proper to address matters of local concern. (NRS 244.146,
3 268.0035) Sections 1 and 2 of this bill require each governing body of a county or
4 incorporated city to adopt an ordinance requiring, with certain exceptions, the
5 owner or operator of a kennel, an animal rescue organization that handles pets or a
6 commercial establishment engaged in the business of handling pets to complete a
7 training course on handling pets prescribed by the governing body. Sections 1 and
8 2 also require the ordinance adopted by the governing body of the county or
9 incorporated city to prescribe the manner in which a kennel, an animal rescue
10 organization that handles pets or a commercial establishment engaged in the
11 business of handling pets: (1) completes the required training course on handling
12 pets; and (2) renews the training biennially. Sections 1 and 2 define the term “pet”
13 to mean a domestic cat or dog commonly kept for pleasure.

THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 244 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. Except as otherwise provided in subsection 3, each board
4 of county commissioners shall adopt an ordinance requiring the
5 owner or operator of a kennel, an animal rescue organization that
6 handles pets or a commercial establishment engaged in the
7 business of handling pets, including, without limitation, a breeder,
8 a cattery, a retailer, a pet groomer or a dealer, to complete a
9 training course on handling pets in a manner prescribed by the
10 board of county commissioners.
11 2. Each owner or operator who is required to complete a
12 training course on handling pets pursuant to subsection 1 shall
13 renew the training biennially in a manner prescribed by the board
14 of county commissioners.
15 3. The ordinance adopted by a board of county
16 commissioners pursuant to subsection 1 does not apply to:
17 (a) A veterinarian;
18 (b) An animal shelter; or
19 (c) A nonprofit organization whose primary purpose is to
20 shelter the pets of victims of domestic violence.
21 4. As used in this section:
22 (a) “Animal rescue organization” has the meaning ascribed to
23 it in NRS 574.205.
24 (b) “Animal shelter” has the meaning ascribed to it in
25 NRS 574.240.
26 (c) “Breeder” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.245.
27 (d) “Cattery” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.250.
28 (e) “Dealer” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.260.
29 (f) “Kennel” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.280.
30 (g) “Nonprofit organization” has the meaning ascribed to it in
31 NRS 82A.060.
32 (h) “Pet” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.300.
33 (i) “Pet groomer” means a person who professionally styles
34 pets for compensation. The term includes, without limitation, a
35 person who professionally bathes, brushes or clips a pet.
36 (j) “Retailer” has the meaning ascribed to it in NRS 574.320.
37 (k) “Veterinarian” has the meaning ascribed to it in
38 NRS 574.330.
39 Sec. 2. Chapter 268 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
40 thereto a new section to read as follows:
41 1. Except as otherwise provided in subsection 3, each city
42 council or other governing body of an incorporated city shall
