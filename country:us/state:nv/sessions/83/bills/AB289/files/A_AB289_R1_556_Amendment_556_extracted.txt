AN ACT relating to education; authorizing the Board of Regents of the University
of Nevada to establish a course of study in financial literacy; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Article 11 of the Nevada Constitution requires the Nevada Legislature to provide for the
2 establishment of a State University that is controlled by a Board of Regents whose duties are
3 prescribed by law. (Nev. Const. Art. 11, § 4) Existing law establishes the Nevada System of
4 Higher Education, which consists of the State University and certain other educational
5 institutions, programs and operations. (NRS 396.020) Existing law authorizes the Board of
6 Regents of the University of Nevada to prescribe the course of study for the System. (NRS
7 396.440) This bill authorizes the Board of Regents to establish a course of study in financial
8 literacy. If established, the course of study must include instruction relating to: (1)
9 preparing a budget; (2) developing a financial plan; (3) the concept of opportunity cost
10 with respect to spending and saving decisions; (4) preparing and filing tax returns; (5)
11 the use of credit and the incurrence of debt; and (6) applying for and receiving financial
12 aid.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 396 of NRS is hereby amended by adding thereto a new
2 section to read as follows:
3 The Board of Regents may establish a course of study in financial literacy. If
4 established, the course of study must include, without limitation, instruction
5 relating to:

3 3. The concept of opportunity cost with respect to spending and saving
