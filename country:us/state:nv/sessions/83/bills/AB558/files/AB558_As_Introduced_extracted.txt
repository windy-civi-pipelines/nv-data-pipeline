Section 1:
EXEMPT
A.B. 558
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 14, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to restore the balances in the
Stale Claims Account, the Emergency Account and
the Contingency Account. (BDR S-1171)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to restore the balances in the Stale
Claims Account, the Emergency Account and the
Contingency Account; and providing other matters
properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
2 Fund to restore the balance in the Stale Claims Account created by
3 NRS 353.097 the sum of $4,616,752.
4 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
5 Fund to restore the balance in the Emergency Account created by
6 NRS 353.263 the sum of $17,000.
7 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General
8 Fund to restore the balance in the Contingency Account created by
9 NRS 353.266 the sum of $17,530,726.
10 Sec. 4. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
H
