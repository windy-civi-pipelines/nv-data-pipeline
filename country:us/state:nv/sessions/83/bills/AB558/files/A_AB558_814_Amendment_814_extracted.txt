ASSEMBLY BILL NO. 558–COMMITTEE ON WAYS AND MEANS
(ON BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF FINANCE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE GOVERNOR)
MAY 14, 2025
_______________
Referred to Committee on Ways and Means
SUMMARY—Makes appropriations to restore the balances in the Stale Claims
Account, the Emergency Account and the Contingency Account.
(BDR S-1171)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation included in
Executive Budget.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making appropriations to restore the balances in the Stale Claims
Account, the Emergency Account and the Contingency Account; and
providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 restore the balance in the Stale Claims Account created by NRS 353.097 the sum of
3 $4,616,752.
4 Sec. 2. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to restore
5 the balance in the Emergency Account created by NRS 353.263 the sum of
6 $17,000.
7 Sec. 3. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to restore
8 the balance in the Contingency Account created by NRS 353.266 the sum of
9 [$17,530,726.] $25,730,726.
10 Sec. 4. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
