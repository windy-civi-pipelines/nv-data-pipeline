SUMMARY—Makes an appropriation to Communities In Schools of Nevada to

FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Contains Appropriation not included in
Executive Budget.

EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT making an appropriation to Communities In Schools of Nevada to provide
integrated student support services to pupils enrolled in public schools
in this State; and providing other matters properly relating thereto.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State General Fund to
2 Communities In Schools of Nevada the sum of [$4,000,000] $1,000,000 for the
3 purpose of providing integrated support services, including, without limitation,
4 services related to academics, basic needs, physical and mental health and social
5 and life skills to pupils enrolled in public schools in this State.
6 2. Upon acceptance of the money appropriated by subsection 1, Communities
7 In Schools of Nevada agrees to:
8 (a) Prepare and transmit a report to the Interim Finance Committee on or
9 before December 18, 2026, that describes each expenditure made from the money
10 appropriated by subsection 1 from the date on which the money was received by
11 Communities In Schools of Nevada through December 1, 2026;
12 (b) Prepare and transmit a final report to the Interim Finance Committee on or
13 before September 17, 2027, that describes each expenditure made from the money
14 appropriated by subsection 1 from the date on which the money was received by
15 Communities In Schools of Nevada through June 30, 2027; and
16 (c) Upon request of the Legislative Commission, make available to the
17 Legislative Auditor any of the books, accounts, claims, reports, vouchers or other
18 records of information, confidential or otherwise, of Communities In Schools of
19 Nevada, regardless of their form or location, that the Legislative Auditor deems
20 necessary to conduct an audit of the use of the money appropriated by subsection 1.
21 3. Any remaining balance of the appropriation made by subsection 1 must not
22 be committed for expenditure after June 30, 2027, by the entity to which the
23 appropriation is made or any entity to which money from the appropriation is
24 granted or otherwise transferred in any manner, and any portion of the appropriated
Senate Amendment No. 912 to Senate Bill No. 135 Page 4
1 money remaining must not be spent for any purpose after September 17, 2027, by
2 either the entity to which the money was appropriated or the entity to which the
3 money was subsequently granted or transferred, and must be reverted to the State
4 General Fund on or before September 17, 2027.
5 Sec. 2. This act becomes effective upon passage and approval.
