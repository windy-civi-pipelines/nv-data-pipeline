Section 1:
S.J.R. 1
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION NO. 1–SENATOR KRASNER
PREFILED JANUARY 13, 2025
____________
SUMMARY—Urges Congress to enact legislation allowing the
surviving spouse of a veteran to receive the military
retirement pay of the veteran after the veteran’s
death. (BDR R-55)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: No.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION—Urging Congress to enact
legislation allowing the surviving spouse of a veteran to
receive the military retirement pay of the veteran after the
veteran’s death.
1 WHEREAS, Certain veterans who serve in the Armed Forces of
2 the United States for a period of at least 20 years or who qualify to
3 retire medically are eligible to receive military retirement pay for
4 their military service; and
5 WHEREAS, Military retirement pay, however, terminates upon
6 the death of the veteran; and
7 WHEREAS, The cessation of military retirement pay may leave
8 the surviving spouse of a veteran in a dire financial situation shortly
9 after the death of the veteran; and
10 WHEREAS, The spouses of veterans hold unique and important
11 roles in upholding the strength of the United States as they provide
12 crucial support to their veteran spouses during the veteran’s military
13 service and retirement; and
14 WHEREAS, Allowing the surviving spouse of a veteran to
15 receive the veteran’s military retirement pay after the veteran’s
16 death recognizes the many sacrifices that a military spouse also
17 makes in service of this country, including delaying or relinquishing
18 personal and professional aspirations; now, therefore, be it
-
*SJR1*
– 2 –
1 RESOLVED BY THE SENATE AND ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF
2 NEVADA, JOINTLY, That the members of the 83rd Session of the
3 Nevada Legislature hereby urge Congress to enact legislation
4 allowing the surviving spouse of a veteran to receive the military
5 retirement pay of the veteran after the veteran’s death; and be it
6 further
7 RESOLVED, That the Secretary of the Senate prepare and
8 transmit a copy of this resolution to the Vice President of the United
9 States as the presiding officer of the United States Senate, the
10 Speaker of the United States House of Representatives and each
11 member of the Nevada Congressional Delegation; and be it further
12 RESOLVED, That this resolution becomes effective upon
13 passage.
H
