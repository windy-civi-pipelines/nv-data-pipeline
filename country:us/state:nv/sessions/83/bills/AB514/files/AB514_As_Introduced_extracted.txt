Section 1:
A.B. 514
HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES
(ON BEHALF OF THE JOINT INTERIM STANDING COMMITTEE
ON HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES)
MARCH 24, 2025
____________
Referred to Committee on Health and Human Services
SUMMARY—Provides for Medicaid coverage of certain mental
health services. (BDR 38-350)
FISCAL NOTE: Effect on Local Government: No.
Effect on the State: Yes.
~
EXPLANATION – Matter in bolded italics is new; matter between brackets [omitted material] is material to be omitted.
AN ACT relating to Medicaid; requiring Medicaid to provide
coverage for certain community-based living arrangement
services; and providing other matters properly relating
thereto.
Legislative Counsel’s Digest:
1 Existing law requires the Department of Health and Human Services to
2 administer Medicaid. (NRS 422.270) Section 1 of this bill requires the Director of
3 the Department to include under Medicaid coverage for care provided through a
4 therapeutic group home to recipients of Medicaid who have serious mental
5 illnesses. Section 1 defines the term “therapeutic group home” to mean a provider
6 of community-based living arrangement services that supports independent,
7 community-based living for persons with serious mental illnesses. Section 2 of this
8 bill makes a conforming change to require the Director to administer the provisions
9 of section 1 in the same manner as the provisions of existing law governing the
10 State Plan for Medicaid.
THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF NEVADA, REPRESENTED IN
SENATE AND ASSEMBLY, DO ENACT AS FOLLOWS:
1 Section 1. Chapter 422 of NRS is hereby amended by adding
2 thereto a new section to read as follows:
3 1. To the extent that federal financial participation is
4 available, the Director shall include under Medicaid coverage for
-
*AB514*
– 2 –
1 care provided through a therapeutic group home to recipients of
2 Medicaid who have serious mental illnesses.
3 2. The Department shall:
4 (a) Establish a method for providing the coverage described in
5 subsection 1.
6 (b) Apply to the Secretary of Health and Human Services for
7 any waiver of federal law or apply for any amendment of the State
8 Plan for Medicaid that is necessary for the Department to receive
9 federal funding to provide the coverage described in subsection 1.
10 (c) Fully cooperate in good faith with the Federal Government
11 during the application process to satisfy the requirements of the
12 Federal Government for obtaining a waiver or amendment
13 pursuant to paragraph (b).
14 3. As used in this section:
15 (a) “Community-based living arrangement services” has the
16 meaning ascribed to it in NRS 449.0026.
17 (b) “Therapeutic group home” means a provider of
18 community-based living arrangement services that supports
19 independent, community-based living for persons with serious
20 mental illnesses.

2 divisions.
3 (c) Shall administer any state program for persons with

11 copy of the plan to the Governor and the Legislature at the

26 Department to communicate effectively with the Federal

36 (f) Has such other powers and duties as are provided by law.
37 2. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Director, or
38 the Director’s designee, is responsible for appointing and removing
39 subordinate officers and employees of the Department.

41 approval.
