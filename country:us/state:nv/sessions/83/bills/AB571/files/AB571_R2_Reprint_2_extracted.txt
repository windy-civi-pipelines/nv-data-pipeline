3 Sec. 5.5. 1. There is hereby appropriated from the State
4 General Fund to the Department of Education the sum of
5 $4,000,000 for the Other State Education Programs budget account
6 for the purpose of creating a grant program to allow teachers and
7 specialized instructional support personnel to obtain necessary
8 supplies and materials for their classrooms or school operations,
9 respectively.
10 2. Of the sum appropriated by subsection 1, the Department
11 shall not expend more than $2,000,000 in Fiscal Year 2025-2026.
12 3. The Department shall enter into an agreement with one or
13 more organizations to provide such organizations with a grant from
14 the Department to administer a program whereby the organizations
15 expend grant money to directly provide teachers and specialized
16 instructional support personnel with necessary supplies and
17 materials for their classrooms or school operations, respectively, in
18 response to a request from such persons.
19 4. Except as otherwise provided in this subsection, an
20 agreement made pursuant to subsection 3 must require that an
21 organization that receives grant money expend not more than $500
22 per individual teacher or specialized instructional support personnel
23 following a request made pursuant to subsection 3. An individual
24 teacher or specialized instructional support personnel may request
25 that the organization expend money in excess of $500 and, to the
26 extent that money from a source other than the grant program
27 established pursuant to this section is available, such a request may
28 be fulfilled.

30 personnel” includes persons employed by a school to provide

40 (f) A school library media specialist; and
41 (g) Any other qualified professional.
